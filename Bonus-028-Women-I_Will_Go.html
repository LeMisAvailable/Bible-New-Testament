I Will Go

Rebekah's Faith Journey - Genesis 24

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Adventurous Folk

[Verse 1]

A servant prayed by the well that day,
"Show me the one," he began to pray,
"The girl who offers water for me and my camels too,
Let her be the one You've chosen true."
Before he finished, I walked that way,
And offered water without delay.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

"Will you go with this man?" my family asked,
A journey to the unknown, a daunting task,
Leave everything I knew, my home, my land,
Trust a God I barely understand.
"I will go," I said with courage new,
Following where God was leading to.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Sometimes God calls us to go,
Into the places we don't know,
Say yes, take that step of faith,
He'll meet you in that sacred space.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
