Children of Light

1 Thessalonians Chapter 5

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

The day of the Lord comes like a thief at night,
While darkness sleeps, we stand in the light,
We are not of night or of the darkness here,
Sons and daughters of day, we have no fear.
Let us be sober, wearing faith and love,
The helmet of salvation from above.

[Chorus]

Rejoice always, never cease to pray,
Give thanks in everything throughout the day,
This is God's will for you in Christ the Lord,
Hold fast to good, embrace His Word.

[Verse 2]

Respect those who labor and lead in the Lord,
Esteem them in love, living in accord,
Warn the idle, encourage the faint,
Help the weak without complaint.
Be patient with all, repay none with wrong,
Pursue what is good, make mercy your song.

[Chorus]

Rejoice always, never cease to pray,
Give thanks in everything throughout the day,
This is God's will for you in Christ the Lord,
Hold fast to good, embrace His Word.

[Bridge]

Do not quench the Spirit's holy fire,
Test all things, reach ever higher,
The God of peace will sanctify you whole,
Spirit, body, blameless soul.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
