New Heaven and New Earth

Revelation Chapter 21

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Uplifting Hope Anthem

[Verse 1]

I saw a new heaven and a new earth arise,
The former things had passed before my eyes,
The holy city, new Jerusalem came down,
A bride adorned, prepared to wear her crown.
God's dwelling now is here among His own,
He wipes each tear, no more shall sorrow be known.

[Chorus]

No more crying, no more pain,
Death is conquered, love will reign,
Behold, He makes all things brand new,
The Alpha, Omega, faithful and true.

[Verse 2]

The city shines with glory like a gem,
Jasper walls around Jerusalem,
Twelve gates of pearl, foundations decked with stone,
The Lamb Himself is light upon the throne.
No temple there, for God and Lamb are all,
The nations walk in light that cannot fall.

[Chorus]

No more crying, no more pain,
Death is conquered, love will reign,
Behold, He makes all things brand new,
The Alpha, Omega, faithful and true.

[Bridge]

The one who overcomes inherits all,
God's sons and daughters, answering His call,
Come, take the water of life so free,
Forever home in eternity.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
