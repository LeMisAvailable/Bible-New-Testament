A King's Regret

Saul - The First King of Israel - 1 Samuel 9-31

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Melancholic Rock Ballad

[Verse 1]

I was searching for my father's donkeys,
When the prophet found me there,
"You're the one God's chosen as king,"
But I hid—I didn't dare.
They pulled me from among the baggage,
The tallest one in all the land.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Samuel said, "Obedience is better,
Than all the sacrifices you could give,"
The Lord has torn the kingdom from you,
This is not how kings should live.
I chased David through the wilderness,
Jealousy became my throne.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

I started humble, ended broken,
A warning for all everywhere,
Learn from me, stay humble, stay true,
Don't throw your calling away.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
