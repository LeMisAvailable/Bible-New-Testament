Grace to the Worst of Sinners

1 Timothy Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

To Timothy, my true child in the faith,
Grace, mercy, and peace—treasures we embrace,
Stay in Ephesus and charge them not to teach,
Different doctrines that are far beyond our reach.
The goal of our instruction is love sincere,
From a pure heart and faith that has no fear.

[Chorus]

Christ Jesus came to save the lost and broken,
I was the worst of sinners, yet He's spoken,
Mercy found me—ignorant, unbelieving,
Now His grace flows, endless and redeeming.

[Verse 2]

The law is good if one uses it right,
Made for the lawless, walking in the night,
For sinners and the unholy, those who stray,
Sound doctrine leads us to the narrow way.
I was a blasphemer, persecutor too,
But mercy came because I did not know truth.

[Chorus]

Christ Jesus came to save the lost and broken,
I was the worst of sinners, yet He's spoken,
Mercy found me—ignorant, unbelieving,
Now His grace flows, endless and redeeming.

[Bridge]

To the King of ages, immortal, unseen,
The only God, let honor forever stream,
Fight the good fight, hold faith and conscience clear,
Timothy, my son, keep the calling dear.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
