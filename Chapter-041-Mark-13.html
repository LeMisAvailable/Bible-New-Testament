Signs of the End Times

Mark Chapter 13

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Epic Prophetic

[Verse 1]

Not one stone upon another shall remain,
Wars and rumors bringing sorrow and pain,
Nation rises against nation in the night,
Earthquakes, famines—but hold on tight.
These are only birth pangs of the age,
The story's written on heaven's page.

[Chorus]

Watch and pray, for you don't know the hour,
The Son of Man returns in glory and power,
Heaven and earth may fade away,
But My words will never decay,
Watch and pray, for you don't know the hour.

[Verse 2]

The sun grows dark, the moon won't shine,
Stars will fall as a prophetic sign,
Then they'll see the Son of Man appear,
With clouds of glory, the end draws near.
Angels gather the elect from every place,
From the ends of earth to see His face.

[Chorus]

Watch and pray, for you don't know the hour,
The Son of Man returns in glory and power,
Heaven and earth may fade away,
But My words will never decay,
Watch and pray, for you don't know the hour.

[Bridge]

Like the fig tree showing signs of spring,
Know the season, hear the angels sing,
Stay awake, don't let your heart grow cold,
The Master's coming—be bold, be bold!

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
