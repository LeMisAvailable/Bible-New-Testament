Lazarus, Come Forth

John Chapter 11

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Lazarus lay sick, his sisters cried,
"Lord, the one You love has died,"
Jesus waited, two more days,
His timing follows higher ways.
"This sickness will not end in death,
But glorify with every breath."

[Chorus]

I am the resurrection and the life,
Believe in Me through every strife,
Though you die, yet shall you live,
Eternal hope is Mine to give.

[Verse 2]

Martha ran to meet the Lord,
"If You'd been here," her heart outpoured,
Mary wept at Jesus' feet,
And Jesus wept, His love complete.
Four days dead, the stone rolled away,
"Lazarus, come forth!" He called that day.

[Chorus]

I am the resurrection and the life,
Believe in Me through every strife,
Though you die, yet shall you live,
Eternal hope is Mine to give.

[Bridge]

Bound in grave clothes, he walked out,
"Unbind him!" came the joyful shout,
Many believed that very hour,
Witnesses to heaven's power.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
