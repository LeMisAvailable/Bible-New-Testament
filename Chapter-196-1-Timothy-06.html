Fight the Good Fight

1 Timothy Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Godliness with contentment is great gain,
We brought nothing in—nothing will remain,
Food and clothing, let these be enough,
The love of money makes the journey rough.
Those who desire riches fall into a snare,
Into temptation and destruction's lair.

[Chorus]

Fight the good fight of the faith today,
Take hold of eternal life along the way,
Pursue righteousness, godliness, and love,
Steadfastness and gentleness from above.

[Verse 2]

But you, O man of God, flee from these things,
Run to the truth that lasting freedom brings,
Keep the commandment unstained until that day,
When Christ appears in His glorious display.
The blessed and only Sovereign, King of kings,
Dwelling in light no mortal ever brings.

[Chorus]

Fight the good fight of the faith today,
Take hold of eternal life along the way,
Pursue righteousness, godliness, and love,
Steadfastness and gentleness from above.

[Bridge]

Charge the rich not to set their hope on wealth,
But on God who gives us life and health,
To do good, to be generous and share,
Storing up treasure beyond compare.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
