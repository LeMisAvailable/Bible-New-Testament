Greetings in His Name

Romans Chapter 16

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Greet Priscilla and Aquila dear,
Co-workers in Christ, ever near,
They risked their lives for Paul's own sake,
The churches thank them for love's sake.
Greet Mary who worked hard for you,
And Andronicus and Junia too.

[Chorus]

To Him who is able to establish you,
By the gospel preached, forever true,
The mystery revealed through ages past,
To the only wise God, glory will last.

[Verse 2]

Watch out for those who cause divide,
Who put obstacles in your stride,
They serve their appetites, not the Lord,
With flattery they spread discord.
Be wise in what is good and right,
And innocent in evil's sight.

[Chorus]

To Him who is able to establish you,
By the gospel preached, forever true,
The mystery revealed through ages past,
To the only wise God, glory will last.

[Bridge]

The God of peace will crush the foe,
Beneath your feet, Satan brought low,
The grace of Jesus Christ be yours,
His love forever more endures.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
