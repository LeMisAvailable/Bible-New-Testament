Blessed Are You

Luke Chapter 6 - Beatitudes and Love Your Enemies

Scripture: Luke 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

We will make this audio available soon

[Intro]

We learn truth from the sacred Word,
Gospel of Luke, chapter six is heard,
Disciples came to follow Him,
The blessings flow up to the brim,
Love enemies, don't grow so grim.

[Verse 1]

Blessed are all you who are poor,
For heaven's kingdom is in store,
Blessed are you who hunger, weep,
Your satisfaction runs soul-deep,
And when they hate you for My name,
Heaven's reward will be your claim.

[Chorus]

So love your enemies with grace,
And bless the ones who curse your face,
Be as merciful as God above,
For this is revolution's love.

[Verse 2]

Do not judge or you'll be judged,
Forgive and then your heart is nudged,
A good tree bears the fruit that's true,
From the heart flows what's in you,
Why the speck in brother's eye,
When your plank still blocks the sky?

[Chorus]

So love your enemies with grace,
And bless the ones who curse your face,
Be as merciful as God above,
For this is revolution's love.

[Bridge]

So build your house upon the rock,
And when the floods come, stand the shock,
Be not just hearers of the Word,
Be the wisest builders ever heard.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
I trust Your heart and all You've planned.

[Chorus]

Amen.

[Pause]
