In The Beginning

Adam - The First Man - Genesis 1-3

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Intimate Acoustic Worship

[Verse 1]

You formed me from the dust of the ground,
Breathed life into my lungs with holy sound,
I opened my eyes for the very first time,
And heard creation's song, so pure, sublime.
You placed me in a garden of wonder,
Named every creature by my side.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

But I reached for what You told me not to take,
And paradise was lost for my mistake,
I hid from You among the trees,
Ashamed, afraid, down on my knees.
Yet even in my brokenness,
You promised One would make things right.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

The first Adam fell from grace,
But the last Adam took my place,
Jesus came to break the curse,
Turn my worst into His best.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
