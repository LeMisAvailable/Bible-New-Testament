The Unknown God

Acts Chapter 17

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In Thessalonica they searched the scrolls,
The message reaching hungry souls,
But jealous hearts stirred up the crowd,
And drove them out with voices loud.
To Berea where nobler minds would dwell,
They examined Scripture, loving it well.

[Chorus]

The God unknown now has a name,
He made the world and still remains,
In Him we live and move and breathe,
He's not far off for those who believe.

[Verse 2]

Paul walked through Athens, grieved in soul,
Idols everywhere took their toll,
At Mars Hill where the thinkers stood,
He preached the risen God so good.
"To an Unknown God" the altar read,
Now I declare Him, raised from the dead.

[Chorus]

The God unknown now has a name,
He made the world and still remains,
In Him we live and move and breathe,
He's not far off for those who believe.

[Bridge]

We are His offspring, fashioned by His hand,
He calls all people in every land,
To turn from empty ways and find,
The risen Lord who frees the mind.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
