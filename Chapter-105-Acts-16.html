Chains Will Break

Acts Chapter 16

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Timothy joined the mission, young and true,
The Spirit led them somewhere fresh and new,
By the river Lydia heard the Word,
Her heart was opened by what she heard.
Purple cloth merchant became a saint,
Her household baptized without restraint.

[Chorus]

At midnight in the prison, praise will rise,
Songs of worship shaking darkened skies,
The earth will tremble, every chain will break,
Believe in Jesus for salvation's sake.

[Verse 2]

Beaten, bleeding, locked in chains so tight,
Paul and Silas sang into the night,
The prisoners listened to their hymns of praise,
Then God moved in His mysterious ways.
The jailer trembling fell before their feet,
"What must I do?" his cry was bittersweet.

[Chorus]

At midnight in the prison, praise will rise,
Songs of worship shaking darkened skies,
The earth will tremble, every chain will break,
Believe in Jesus for salvation's sake.

[Bridge]

Believe on the Lord and you shall be saved,
You and your household from the grave,
That very hour he washed their wounds with care,
And found redemption in the midnight air.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
