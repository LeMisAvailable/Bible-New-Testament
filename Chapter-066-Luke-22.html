The Night of Surrender

Luke Chapter 22

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Satan entered Judas' heart,
Thirty silver coins his part,
The Passover was drawing near,
The upper room, disciples here.
"This is My body, broken, free,
This cup, My blood, remember Me."

[Chorus]

Not my will, but Yours be done,
In the garden, the battle's won,
Sweat like blood upon His face,
Yet He chose to take our place.
Betrayed with a kiss of shame,
Still, He loved us all the same.

[Verse 2]

"Who is greatest?" they debated,
But the servant is most sacred,
"Simon, Satan seeks to sift,
But I have prayed—your faith won't drift.
Before the rooster crows tonight,
Three times you'll deny My light."

[Chorus]

Not my will, but Yours be done,
In the garden, the battle's won,
Sweat like blood upon His face,
Yet He chose to take our place.
Betrayed with a kiss of shame,
Still, He loved us all the same.

[Bridge]

An angel came to give Him strength,
As He prayed at greatest length,
The crowd arrived with swords and hate,
He healed the ear they meant to take.
Peter wept in bitter tears,
The rooster's cry confirmed his fears.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
