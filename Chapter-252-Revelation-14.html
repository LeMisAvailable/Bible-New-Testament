The Lamb and 144,000

Revelation Chapter 14

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Triumphant Worship Anthem

[Verse 1]

Upon Mount Zion the Lamb stands tall,
With a hundred forty-four thousand who answered the call,
His Father's name written upon their brow,
Redeemed from the earth, before Him they bow.
A new song rings like rushing waters deep,
A melody only the faithful can keep.

[Chorus]

Fear God and give Him glory, the hour has come,
Worship the Maker, the Holy One,
Three angels fly with eternal decree,
The gospel of hope for all who believe.

[Verse 2]

Fallen, fallen is Babylon the great,
She made nations drink from her cup of hate,
But blessed are the dead who die in the Lord,
Their labors cease, they receive their reward.
The harvest is ripe, the earth's grapes are full,
The Son of Man comes, majestic and powerful.

[Chorus]

Fear God and give Him glory, the hour has come,
Worship the Maker, the Holy One,
Three angels fly with eternal decree,
The gospel of hope for all who believe.

[Bridge]

These are the pure, the firstfruits to God,
No lie on their lips, they follow where He trod,
Blameless before the throne of grace,
Forever to dwell in His embrace.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
