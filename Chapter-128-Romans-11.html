The Olive Tree

Romans Chapter 11

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Did God reject His people dear?
By no means, wipe away that fear,
A remnant chosen still remains,
By grace alone, not human strains.
Israel stumbled but did not fall,
Salvation came to reach us all.

[Chorus]

O the depth of wisdom so divine,
The riches of His grand design,
Who has known the mind of God above?
From Him and through Him flows His love.

[Verse 2]

You wild olive branch grafted in,
Do not boast against the kin,
You stand by faith, so do not be proud,
The root supports you through the crowd.
If natural branches were cut away,
Be humble, walk in faith each day.

[Chorus]

O the depth of wisdom so divine,
The riches of His grand design,
Who has known the mind of God above?
From Him and through Him flows His love.

[Bridge]

All Israel will be saved one day,
The Deliverer will turn away,
The sins of Jacob, make them whole,
God's gifts and calling fill the soul.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
