Seeds and Soil

Matthew Chapter 13

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Folk Acoustic

[Verse 1]

A farmer went out to scatter his seed,
Some fell on the path—the birds came to feed,
Some fell on rocks where the soil was thin,
Sprang up quick but withered within.
Some fell among thorns that choked out the life,
But good soil brought harvest through every strife.

[Chorus]

The kingdom of heaven is like a seed so small,
The mustard tree grows and shelters them all,
Like treasure hidden, like a pearl of great price,
Sell everything you have—pay any sacrifice.

[Verse 2]

Wheat and weeds grow together in the field,
At harvest time the truth will be revealed,
The angels will gather the righteous to shine,
Like the sun in the kingdom, eternally divine.
He who has ears, let him hear and understand,
These mysteries of heaven from God's own hand.

[Chorus]

The kingdom of heaven is like a seed so small,
The mustard tree grows and shelters them all,
Like treasure hidden, like a pearl of great price,
Sell everything you have—pay any sacrifice.

[Bridge]

Like yeast in dough, the kingdom will spread,
Through every nation, from the living bread,
In His hometown they took offense that day,
A prophet has no honor where he used to play.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
