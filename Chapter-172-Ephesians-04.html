Unity in the Body

Ephesians Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Walk worthy of the calling you've received,
With humility and gentleness believed,
Bearing with one another in love so true,
Eager to keep the unity that binds you.
One body, one Spirit, one hope, one Lord,
One faith, one baptism, one God adored.

[Chorus]

One body, many parts, together we stand,
Built up in love by the Master's hand,
Speaking the truth, growing in grace,
United in Christ, our dwelling place.

[Verse 2]

He gave gifts to build His church on earth,
Apostles, prophets of eternal worth,
Evangelists, pastors, and teachers too,
Equipping the saints for the work to do.
No longer tossed by every wind of lies,
But growing mature to our full size.

[Chorus]

One body, many parts, together we stand,
Built up in love by the Master's hand,
Speaking the truth, growing in grace,
United in Christ, our dwelling place.

[Bridge]

Put off the old self, corrupted by desire,
Be renewed in your mind, lifted higher,
Put on the new self, created to be,
Righteous and holy, finally free.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
