Grace Is Enough

2 Corinthians Chapter 12

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

I know a man caught up to paradise,
Fourteen years ago, a vision beyond price,
He heard things no tongue can tell,
Inexpressible words where glory dwells.
But to keep me humble, a thorn was given,
A messenger to remind me I'm forgiven.

[Chorus]

My grace is enough, it's all that you need,
My power is perfect when you are weak,
So I'll boast in my weakness, I'll glory in pain,
For when I am nothing, Christ is my gain,
His strength is made perfect again and again.

[Verse 2]

Three times I pleaded for relief,
But the Lord's answer shattered my belief,
That strength means never feeling low,
Now in weakness, His power flows.
I delight in hardships, in struggles I face,
For when I am weak, I stand in His grace.

[Chorus]

My grace is enough, it's all that you need,
My power is perfect when you are weak,
So I'll boast in my weakness, I'll glory in pain,
For when I am nothing, Christ is my gain,
His strength is made perfect again and again.

[Bridge]

The signs of an apostle were performed,
With patience through every storm,
Not a burden but a blessing I'll be,
For I seek not yours, but you, willingly.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
