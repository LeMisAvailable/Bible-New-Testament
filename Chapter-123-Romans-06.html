Dead to Sin, Alive in Christ

Romans Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Shall we go on sinning still,
So that grace may overflow and fill?
By no means, we died to sin,
How can we live in it again?
Buried with Him through baptism's wave,
Raised to new life beyond the grave.

[Chorus]

Count yourselves dead to sin's demand,
But alive to God, take His hand,
United with Christ in death and life,
No longer slaves to endless strife.

[Verse 2]

Our old self was crucified,
So the body of sin has died,
We are freed from sin's control,
Christ's resurrection makes us whole.
Do not let sin reign in mortal flesh,
Offer yourselves to God afresh.

[Chorus]

Count yourselves dead to sin's demand,
But alive to God, take His hand,
United with Christ in death and life,
No longer slaves to endless strife.

[Bridge]

The wages of sin is death, we know,
But God's gift is life, eternal flow,
In Christ Jesus, our Lord and King,
To Him alone our praises ring.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
