Living Sacrifice

Romans Chapter 12

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

I urge you by God's mercy true,
Offer your bodies, all of you,
A living sacrifice, holy and pure,
Spiritual worship that will endure.
Do not conform to this world's way,
Be transformed anew each day.

[Chorus]

We are one body, many parts,
Each with different gifts and hearts,
Love must be sincere and real,
Devoted to the bonds we feel.

[Verse 2]

If your gift is prophecy, speak,
If serving, serve the strong and weak,
If teaching, teach with faithful care,
If giving, give beyond compare.
Honor others above yourself,
Put pride and ego on the shelf.

[Chorus]

We are one body, many parts,
Each with different gifts and hearts,
Love must be sincere and real,
Devoted to the bonds we feel.

[Bridge]

Bless those who persecute and hate,
Rejoice with those who celebrate,
Mourn with those who weep and cry,
Overcome evil, lift grace high.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
