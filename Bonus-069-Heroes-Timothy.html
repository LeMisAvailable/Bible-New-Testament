Don't Let Them Look Down

Timothy - Paul's Young Protégé - Acts 16, 1 & 2 Timothy

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Encouraging Pop Rock

[Verse 1]

My grandmother taught me the Scriptures,
My mother showed me how to pray,
When Paul came through my town in Lystra,
He took me with him on his way.
I was young and often afraid,
But Paul saw something more in me.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Paul called me his true son in the faith,
He sent me to churches to lead,
"Guard what's been entrusted to you,
And plant the gospel seed."
"Fan into flame the gift of God in you!
For God didn't give you a spirit of fear."

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Don't let them look down on you because you're young!
Set an example in speech and in love!
Fight the good fight of faith, take hold of life!
You're exactly where you should be—be strong!

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
