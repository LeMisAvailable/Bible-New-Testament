False Teachers

2 Peter Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

False prophets arose among the people then,
False teachers now bring heresies again,
Denying the Master who bought them with His blood,
Many follow their destructive ways like flood.
In their greed they exploit with words untrue,
But their judgment waits, their destruction's due.

[Chorus]

The Lord knows how to rescue godly souls,
From trials that test them and make them whole,
He keeps the unrighteous under punishment's hand,
Until the day of judgment as He planned.

[Verse 2]

God spared not the angels when they fell from grace,
Nor the ancient world, but saved Noah's place,
He condemned the cities of Sodom to ash,
But rescued Lot from judgment's fiery flash.
Bold and arrogant, they blaspheme what they don't know,
Like irrational creatures, reaping what they sow.

[Chorus]

The Lord knows how to rescue godly souls,
From trials that test them and make them whole,
He keeps the unrighteous under punishment's hand,
Until the day of judgment as He planned.

[Bridge]

They promise freedom while they're slaves to sin,
Springs without water, mist the storm drives in,
Better never known the way of righteousness,
Than turn from the holy command and transgress.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
