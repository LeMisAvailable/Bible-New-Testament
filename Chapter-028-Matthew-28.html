He Is Risen

Matthew Chapter 28

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Triumphant Celebration

[Verse 1]

At dawn on the first day, the women came,
The earth shook, and nothing was the same,
An angel descended, rolled the stone away,
The guards fell like dead men where they lay.
"Do not be afraid," the angel spoke,
"He is not here—death's chains are broke!"

[Chorus]

He is risen, just as He said,
Come and see where He once lay dead,
Go and tell the disciples now,
Death is conquered, to the risen Lord bow!

[Verse 2]

With fear and joy, they ran to tell,
Jesus met them—"Greetings!"—all was well,
They clasped His feet and worshiped there,
The risen Lord, alive and fair.
"Go to Galilee, I'll meet you soon,"
The morning star outshining the moon.

[Chorus]

He is risen, just as He said,
Come and see where He once lay dead,
Go and tell the disciples now,
Death is conquered, to the risen Lord bow!

[Bridge]

"All authority is given to Me,
In heaven and earth, for all to see,
Go and make disciples of every nation,
Baptize and teach—this is your station."

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
