Promises Proclaimed

Luke Chapter 1 - Birth Announcements of John and Jesus

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In the temple, incense rising high,
Zechariah lifted prayers to the sky,
An angel spoke through holy flame,
"Your son will come, and John's his name."
Elizabeth will bear a child at last,
The years of waiting finally passed.

[Chorus]

Heaven bends to touch the earth tonight,
Angels herald the approaching light,
Two mothers chosen, two sons foretold,
A story of redemption now unfolds.

[Verse 2]

In Nazareth, a virgin knelt in prayer,
Gabriel descended through the air,
"Rejoice, O favored one," he cried,
"The Holy Spirit will be your guide."
Mary trembled, yet her faith stood tall,
"I am the Lord's, let it befall."

[Chorus]

Heaven bends to touch the earth tonight,
Angels herald the approaching light,
Two mothers chosen, two sons foretold,
A story of redemption now unfolds.

[Bridge]

My soul magnifies the Lord on high,
My spirit soars, I cannot deny,
He lifts the lowly, fills the poor,
His mercy lasts forevermore.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
