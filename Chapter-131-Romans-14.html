Do Not Judge

Romans Chapter 14

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Accept the one whose faith is weak,
Without disputes on things they seek,
One eats all things, another just plants,
Neither should judge the other's stance.
Who are you to judge someone,
When they belong to God alone?

[Chorus]

We live and die unto the Lord,
Whether living or dying, we're His reward,
So why do you judge your sister or brother?
We all will stand before the Father.

[Verse 2]

One regards a day as holy ground,
Another sees all days as sound,
Let each be convinced in their own mind,
Leave your judgments far behind.
The kingdom is not food or drink,
But righteousness, peace, joy we think.

[Chorus]

We live and die unto the Lord,
Whether living or dying, we're His reward,
So why do you judge your sister or brother?
We all will stand before the Father.

[Bridge]

Do not destroy by what you eat,
The work of God is far more sweet,
Make every effort to bring peace,
Let mutual building never cease.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
