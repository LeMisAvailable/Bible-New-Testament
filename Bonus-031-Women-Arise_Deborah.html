Arise Deborah

Deborah - Judge and Warrior - Judges 4-5

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Powerful Rock Anthem

[Verse 1]

Under a palm tree I sat judging Israel,
A prophetess leading when the nation fell,
For twenty years Canaan oppressed us with might,
Nine hundred iron chariots—a terrifying sight.
But God spoke to me, said, "Now is the time,
Call Barak to battle, the victory is thine."

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Barak said, "I'll go if you go with me,"
I said, "I'll go, but the glory you won't see,
The honor will go to a woman's hand."
God routed the army across the land.
Sisera fled to Jael's tent that day,
A woman brought victory, come what may.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Arise, arise, you sleeping ones,
The battle belongs to the Lord,
He calls the weak to defeat the strong,
Take up your shield and sword!

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
