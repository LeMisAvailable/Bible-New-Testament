Faith That Echoes

1 Thessalonians Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

We give thanks for you always in prayer,
Your faith and labor, your love beyond compare,
In affliction you received the Word with joy,
A hope so strong that nothing could destroy.
From idols turned to serve the living God,
Walking the path that faithful saints have trod.

[Chorus]

Your faith rings out like thunder across the land,
From Macedonia to shores of distant sand,
The Gospel came in power, not words alone,
In every heart, His glory now is known.

[Verse 2]

You became examples to all who believe,
The Word of the Lord from you we receive,
Waiting for His Son from heaven above,
The One who saves us by His endless love.
From wrath delivered, in grace we stand complete,
Until the day we bow at Jesus' feet.

[Chorus]

Your faith rings out like thunder across the land,
From Macedonia to shores of distant sand,
The Gospel came in power, not words alone,
In every heart, His glory now is known.

[Bridge]

The Holy Spirit moves with fire and might,
Transforming darkness into radiant light,
With deep conviction, souls are turned around,
In Thessalonica, true faith is found.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
