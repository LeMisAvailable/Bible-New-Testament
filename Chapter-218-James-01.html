Joy in Trials

James Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

When the fires of testing surround my soul,
When the trials come to make me whole,
I will count it joy, not sorrow or pain,
For endurance rises like gentle rain.
Let patience complete its perfect work,
In the depths of struggle, don't let faith shirk.

[Chorus]

If any lack wisdom, just ask the Lord,
He gives generously, never ignored,
No doubting waves upon the sea,
Stand firm in faith and you will be free.

[Verse 2]

Every good gift falls from above,
From the Father of lights, wrapped in love,
No shadow of turning, no shifting shade,
His promises never, ever fade.
Be swift to hear and slow to speak,
In humble surrender, His face I seek.

[Chorus]

If any lack wisdom, just ask the Lord,
He gives generously, never ignored,
No doubting waves upon the sea,
Stand firm in faith and you will be free.

[Bridge]

Be doers of the Word, not hearers alone,
Look into perfect law, make it your own,
Pure religion cares for widow and child,
Keep yourself from the world undefiled.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
