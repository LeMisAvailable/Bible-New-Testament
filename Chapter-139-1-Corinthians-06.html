Body as Temple

1 Corinthians Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Why do brothers go to court,
Against each other—wrong resort,
Better to be wronged than fight,
Before the world, show kingdom light,
The saints will judge both earth and sky,
Can we not settle wrongs close by?

[Chorus]

Your body is a temple, holy shrine,
Where the Spirit dwells divine,
You were bought with precious blood,
Honor God with all you love.

[Verse 2]

You were washed, you were made new,
Justified in all you do,
Sanctified by Spirit's power,
Living changed from this hour,
Flee from sin that stains the soul,
Let the Lord now make you whole.

[Chorus]

Your body is a temple, holy shrine,
Where the Spirit dwells divine,
You were bought with precious blood,
Honor God with all you love.

[Bridge]

All things lawful, but not all are wise,
I won't be mastered by what dies,
United with the Lord as one,
In body glorify the Son.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
