Two Witnesses and the Seventh Trumpet

Revelation Chapter 11

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Triumphant Epic Anthem

[Verse 1]

Two witnesses will prophesy in sackcloth, bold and true,
For twelve hundred sixty days, proclaiming what God knew,
Like olive trees and lampstands standing before the Lord,
Fire from their mouths devours all who raise the sword.
They shut the sky from rain and turn the waters red,
Until the beast rises up and leaves them lying dead.

[Chorus]

The kingdom of this world becomes our Lord's domain,
And He shall reign forever, Christ the Lamb once slain,
The seventh trumpet sounds, the heavens thunder praise,
"He shall reign forever through eternal days!"

[Verse 2]

For three and a half days, their bodies lie displayed,
The world rejoices, sending gifts—how dark the price they paid,
But breath from God revived them, they stood upon their feet,
Terror struck the mockers as they watched the dead retreat.
A voice called, "Come up here!" They rose on clouds of white,
An earthquake shook the city at that awesome sight.

[Chorus]

The kingdom of this world becomes our Lord's domain,
And He shall reign forever, Christ the Lamb once slain,
The seventh trumpet sounds, the heavens thunder praise,
"He shall reign forever through eternal days!"

[Bridge]

The temple opened wide in heaven's holy place,
The ark of covenant revealed, lightning filled the space,
The elders fell in worship, crowns before the throne,
"We give You thanks, Almighty God, Your power now is shown!"

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
