The Spirit Will Come

John Chapter 16

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

"It is good that I go away,
For if I stay, the Helper won't come to stay,
But if I go, I'll send Him down,
The Spirit of Truth, wearing heaven's crown."
He'll convict the world of sin,
Righteousness and judgment within.

[Chorus]

Your sorrow will turn to joy,
A gift that the world can't destroy,
Like a woman forgets her pain,
When she holds new life again.

[Verse 2]

"A little while and you won't see Me,
Then a little more and you'll believe,
You'll weep and mourn while the world rejoices,
But your grief will yield to hopeful voices."
Ask the Father in My name,
Joy complete, forever the same.

[Chorus]

Your sorrow will turn to joy,
A gift that the world can't destroy,
Like a woman forgets her pain,
When she holds new life again.

[Bridge]

In this world you'll have trouble and strife,
But take heart, I've conquered this life,
The Father loves you because you believe,
In the Son of God, you will receive.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
