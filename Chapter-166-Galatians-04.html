Sons and Heirs

Galatians Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

When the fullness of time had finally come,
God sent forth His beloved Son,
Born of woman, under law's demand,
To redeem us by His nail-scarred hand.
No longer slaves but children true,
He made us heirs, our lives made new.

[Chorus]

Abba, Father, we cry to You,
Your Spirit in our hearts rings true,
No longer orphans, lost and alone,
We are Your children, called Your own.

[Verse 2]

Why turn back to weak and worthless things,
To slavery when freedom sings?
You observe the days and months and years,
I fear my labor ends in tears.
Remember how you welcomed me,
Would have given your eyes to set me free.

[Chorus]

Abba, Father, we cry to You,
Your Spirit in our hearts rings true,
No longer orphans, lost and alone,
We are Your children, called Your own.

[Bridge]

Two covenants, two mountains stand,
Hagar's bondage, Sarah's promised land,
The Jerusalem above is free,
She is our mother, our destiny.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
