No One Righteous

Romans Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

There is no one righteous, not even one,
No one who seeks God, under the sun,
All have turned away, together lost,
We cannot pay the price sin cost.
Our throats are graves, our tongues deceive,
The poison of vipers in what we believe.

[Chorus]

But now a righteousness has been revealed,
Through faith in Christ, our fate is sealed,
Justified freely by His grace,
Redemption found in His embrace.

[Verse 2]

The law brings knowledge of our sin,
But cannot cleanse the stain within,
For all have sinned and fall so short,
Of glory's throne, heaven's court.
Yet God displayed His sacrifice,
Christ's blood alone paid the price.

[Chorus]

But now a righteousness has been revealed,
Through faith in Christ, our fate is sealed,
Justified freely by His grace,
Redemption found in His embrace.

[Bridge]

Where then is boasting? It is gone,
By works of law? No, faith alone,
One God for Jew and Gentile too,
By faith He makes us all brand new.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
