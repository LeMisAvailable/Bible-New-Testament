Flee Idolatry

1 Corinthians Chapter 10

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Our fathers passed beneath the cloud,
Through parted seas they walked unbowed,
They drank from Christ, the spiritual rock,
Yet many fell—a warning flock,
These things were written for our sake,
To teach us which paths not to take.

[Chorus]

God is faithful, He will not let you fall,
Beyond what you can bear through it all,
With every trial a way to flee,
His grace provides the victory.

[Verse 2]

If you think you stand, beware,
Lest falling find you unaware,
Flee from idols, run from sin,
Let no dark compromise begin,
You cannot drink from two cups at once,
The Lord's table or the demons' response.

[Chorus]

God is faithful, He will not let you fall,
Beyond what you can bear through it all,
With every trial a way to flee,
His grace provides the victory.

[Bridge]

Whether you eat or drink this day,
Do all for glory, come what may,
Cause no one else to stumble down,
Seek others' good—pursue the crown.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
