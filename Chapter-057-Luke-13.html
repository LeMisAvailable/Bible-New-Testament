The Narrow Door

Luke Chapter 13

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

The tower fell, the blood was spilled,
Yet not because their sin was filled,
Beyond the measure of the rest—
Repent, He said, and you'll be blessed.
The fig tree stood without its fruit,
One more year to grow its root.

[Chorus]

Through the narrow door I'll go,
Though the path is hard, I know,
Many seek but few will find,
The gate of grace for humankind.
Lord, I'm striving to break through,
All my hope is found in You.

[Verse 2]

On the Sabbath, bent and bound,
A woman's healing grace had found,
Eighteen years of heavy chains,
Set free from all her bitter pains.
Like a mustard seed so small,
Your kingdom rises over all.

[Chorus]

Through the narrow door I'll go,
Though the path is hard, I know,
Many seek but few will find,
The gate of grace for humankind.
Lord, I'm striving to break through,
All my hope is found in You.

[Bridge]

Jerusalem, Jerusalem,
How often I would gather them,
Like a hen protects her young,
But your hearts remained unsung.
Yet blessed is He who comes to reign,
In the Lord's most holy name.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
