Christ Supreme

Colossians Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

We give thanks to God, the Father above,
Hearing of your faith and your steadfast love,
The hope laid up for you in heaven's store,
The gospel bearing fruit forevermore.
We pray that you may walk worthy of Him,
Bearing fruit, growing strong, not growing dim.

[Chorus]

He is the image of the invisible God,
Firstborn of creation, paths the saints have trod,
In Him all things were made, all things hold fast,
The head of the church, the first and last.

[Verse 2]

He has rescued us from darkness's domain,
Transferred to His kingdom, breaking every chain,
In Him we have redemption, sins forgiven,
The fullness of God in Christ is given.
Through His blood upon the cross He made peace,
Reconciling all things, granting us release.

[Chorus]

He is the image of the invisible God,
Firstborn of creation, paths the saints have trod,
In Him all things were made, all things hold fast,
The head of the church, the first and last.

[Bridge]

The mystery hidden for ages untold,
Now revealed to the saints, more precious than gold,
Christ in you, the hope of glory bright,
Walking in His wisdom, His eternal light.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
