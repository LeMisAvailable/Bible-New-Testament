Test Yourselves

2 Corinthians Chapter 13

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

This will be my third time coming to you,
Every matter established by witnesses true,
I warned before and I warn again,
When I come, I will not spare sin.
You seek proof that Christ speaks through me,
He is not weak but powerful, you'll see.

[Chorus]

Examine yourselves, test your own heart,
Is Jesus Christ living in every part?
Unless you fail the test of faith,
He dwells within, securing your place,
Look inside and see His grace.

[Verse 2]

We pray to God that you do no wrong,
Not to prove ourselves, but to make you strong,
We cannot do anything against the truth,
Only for it, bearing kingdom fruit.
We are glad when we are weak and you are strong,
Your restoration is our prayer all along.

[Chorus]

Examine yourselves, test your own heart,
Is Jesus Christ living in every part?
Unless you fail the test of faith,
He dwells within, securing your place,
Look inside and see His grace.

[Bridge]

Aim for restoration, comfort one another,
Agree and live in peace with your brother,
The God of love and peace will be with you,
Greet each other holy, faithful and true.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
