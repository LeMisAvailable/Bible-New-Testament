The Armor of God

Ephesians Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Children, obey your parents in the Lord,
Honor your father and mother, His word,
Fathers, do not provoke to wrath,
Raise them in the Lord's own path.
Be strong in the Lord, in His mighty power,
Stand firm in the battle's darkest hour.

[Chorus]

Put on the armor of God to stand,
Belt of truth, sword in hand,
Breastplate of righteousness, shield of faith,
Helmet of salvation, standing safe.

[Verse 2]

Our struggle is not against flesh and blood,
But powers of darkness, the devil's flood,
Rulers and authorities in high places reign,
Spiritual forces of wickedness and pain.
Stand firm therefore, gird your waist with truth,
The gospel of peace, the feet of youth.

[Chorus]

Put on the armor of God to stand,
Belt of truth, sword in hand,
Breastplate of righteousness, shield of faith,
Helmet of salvation, standing safe.

[Bridge]

Pray at all times in the Spirit's might,
Be alert, keep watch through the night,
The sword of the Spirit, the Word of God,
Extinguish the arrows, stand where saints have trod.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
