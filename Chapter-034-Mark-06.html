Prophet Without Honor

Mark Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Reflective Indie

[Verse 1]

In His hometown, Jesus began to teach,
But the people doubted—He couldn't reach,
"Isn't this the carpenter's son we know?"
A prophet without honor, faith running low.
He sent the Twelve with power to heal,
Traveling light, making the kingdom real.

[Chorus]

Take nothing but a staff for the road,
Trust the Father to carry your load,
Shake the dust when they turn you away,
Keep moving forward, keep faith today.

[Verse 2]

Herod heard of wonders far and wide,
Guilty conscience couldn't hide inside,
John the Baptist, silenced by the sword,
A righteous voice that Herod ignored.
But the truth lives on, it cannot die,
Even when prophets say goodbye.

[Chorus]

Take nothing but a staff for the road,
Trust the Father to carry your load,
Shake the dust when they turn you away,
Keep moving forward, keep faith today.

[Bridge]

Five thousand fed from a basket small,
Five loaves, two fish—He provided for all,
Walking on water in the fourth watch of night,
"Take courage, it is I"—everything's alright.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
