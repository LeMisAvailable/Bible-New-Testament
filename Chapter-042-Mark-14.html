Gethsemane

Mark Chapter 14

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Somber Ballad

[Verse 1]

A woman came with precious nard,
She broke the flask, she broke His heart,
Anointing Him for burial's day,
Her love poured out would never fade away.
At the table, bread was broken free,
"This is My body, given for thee."

[Chorus]

Not My will, but Yours be done,
Father, if You will, remove this cup,
In Gethsemane, My soul is crushed,
Yet in Your hands, I place my trust,
Not My will, but Yours be done.

[Verse 2]

"One of you will betray Me tonight,"
Judas left beneath the fading light,
In the garden, Jesus knelt to pray,
While His disciples slept the night away.
With a kiss, the Son of Man was sold,
Thirty silver coins, a heart so cold.

[Chorus]

Not My will, but Yours be done,
Father, if You will, remove this cup,
In Gethsemane, My soul is crushed,
Yet in Your hands, I place my trust,
Not My will, but Yours be done.

[Bridge]

Peter swore he'd never deny,
But three times spoke the painful lie,
When the rooster crowed at break of day,
He wept for words he couldn't unsay.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
