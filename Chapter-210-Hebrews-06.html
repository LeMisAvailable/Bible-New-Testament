Maturity, God's Promise Sure

Hebrews Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Inspirational Power Ballad

[Verse 1]

Let us leave the elementary way,
Press on to maturity today,
Not laying again the foundation stone,
Of repentance and faith already known.
This we will do if God permits us to,
Growing deeper in all that's right and true.

[Chorus]

God's promise is an anchor for the soul,
Firm and secure, it makes the broken whole,
He swore by Himself, no greater name to give,
By two unchanging things, in hope we live.

[Verse 2]

Though we speak of warnings, dark and grave,
We are confident of better things you gave,
God is not unjust to forget your love,
The work you've shown reflects His grace above.
We want each of you to show the same,
Diligence and hope that bears His name.

[Chorus]

God's promise is an anchor for the soul,
Firm and secure, it makes the broken whole,
He swore by Himself, no greater name to give,
By two unchanging things, in hope we live.

[Bridge]

This hope enters behind the veil so bright,
Where Jesus went as forerunner of light,
A high priest forever, Melchizedek's line,
Securing our future in love divine.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
