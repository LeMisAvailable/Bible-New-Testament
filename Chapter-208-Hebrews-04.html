Sabbath Rest, Living Word

Hebrews Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Contemplative Worship Ballad

[Verse 1]

The promise of rest still stands today,
Let none of us miss it, fall away,
Good news came to them as it came to us,
But without faith, the message turned to dust.
God rested on the seventh day complete,
A Sabbath rest for all who trust and seek.

[Chorus]

There remains a rest for the people of God,
Cease from your striving, lay down your rod,
The Word is alive, sharper than any blade,
It judges our hearts, nothing can evade.

[Verse 2]

For the Word of God is living, breathing, true,
It pierces soul and spirit through and through,
Dividing joints and marrow, thoughts laid bare,
No creature hidden from His watchful care.
All things are naked, open to His eyes,
Before Him whom we answer, nothing hides.

[Chorus]

There remains a rest for the people of God,
Cease from your striving, lay down your rod,
The Word is alive, sharper than any blade,
It judges our hearts, nothing can evade.

[Bridge]

We have a great High Priest who passed through the skies,
Jesus the Son of God, our eternal prize,
He sympathizes with our weakest hour,
Tempted like us yet standing in power.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
