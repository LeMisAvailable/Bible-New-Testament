Seven Bowls of Wrath

Revelation Chapter 16

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Dramatic Prophetic Anthem

[Verse 1]

A voice thundered forth from the temple high,
"Pour out the bowls of wrath!" came heaven's cry,
Upon the earth, the sea, the rivers wide,
Upon the sun, where scorching flames reside.
Darkness covered the throne of the beast,
Yet hardened hearts refused to find release.

[Chorus]

True and just are Your judgments, Lord divine,
You give them blood to drink, for blood was their crime,
The altar cries, "Yes, Lord Almighty, true!"
Righteous and holy in all that You do.

[Verse 2]

The Euphrates dried, the kings of the east,
Gathered for battle, for Armageddon's feast,
Unclean spirits like frogs deceived the world,
But heaven's banner soon would be unfurled.
"Behold, I come like a thief!" says the Lord,
Blessed are the watchful who keep His word.

[Chorus]

True and just are Your judgments, Lord divine,
You give them blood to drink, for blood was their crime,
The altar cries, "Yes, Lord Almighty, true!"
Righteous and holy in all that You do.

[Bridge]

The seventh bowl poured into the air,
"It is done!" resounded everywhere,
Lightning, thunder, earthquake shook the ground,
Yet in Christ alone, true refuge is found.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
