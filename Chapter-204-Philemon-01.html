No Longer a Slave

Philemon Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Paul, a prisoner of Christ Jesus, writes today,
To Philemon, our beloved friend, I say,
Grace and peace from God the Father above,
I thank my God always for your faith and love.
Your love refreshes hearts of all the saints,
I hear of it, and my spirit never faints.

[Chorus]

No longer as a slave but something more,
A beloved brother, precious to the core,
What was once useless now is of great worth,
In Christ, a new beginning, a rebirth.

[Verse 2]

I appeal to you for my child Onesimus,
Whose father I became in chains for us,
I am sending him back—my very heart,
I wanted to keep him, but we must not part.
Without your consent I would do nothing here,
That your goodness be willing, not from fear.

[Chorus]

No longer as a slave but something more,
A beloved brother, precious to the core,
What was once useless now is of great worth,
In Christ, a new beginning, a rebirth.

[Bridge]

Receive him as you would receive my face,
If he owes you anything, charge it to my place,
I write this with my hand—I will repay,
Refresh my heart in Christ, brother, today.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
