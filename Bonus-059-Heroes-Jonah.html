You Can't Outrun God

Jonah - The Reluctant Prophet - Book of Jonah

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Fun Pop Rock

[Verse 1]

God said, "Jonah, go to Nineveh,"
But I bought a ticket the other way,
I didn't want to preach to my enemies,
I wanted them to pay.
I found a ship and went below,
Thought I could hide from the Lord.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Inside that fish I finally prayed,
"Salvation comes from the Lord!"
The fish spat me out on dry land,
And I heard God's voice once more.
This time I went to Nineveh,
And wouldn't you know—they all repented!

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

You can't outrun God!
No matter how fast you flee!
His mercy can't be caged,
Even Nineveh was saved!

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
