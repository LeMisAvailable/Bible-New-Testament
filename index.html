Welcome to the New Testament Bible and Bible Inspired Songs collection — a creative journey through Scripture set to music.

All 260 chapters of the New Testament have been transformed into original songs, capturing the key themes, stories, and teachings in lyrical form that speaks to the heart.

Designed especially for teenagers, these songs make the Bible accessible, memorable, and deeply engaging for a new generation.

Every song follows a consistent structure — verses, chorus, bridge, and a unifying outro that ties the entire collection together with this reminder:

"When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand."

Whether for personal devotion, youth group study, or family worship, these songs bring the New Testament to life in a fresh and powerful way.

🎵 Musical Training Resource: This collection also serves as excellent vocal and musical training material when paired with scoresheets and karaoke tracks — perfect for choir practice, music education, and developing worship leaders.

A Message of Hope

This music wraps the hurting soul in hope — telling them darkness is not forever and they are not alone. Each word gently whispers: you will be held, healed, and set free.

"Darkness abates, vanquished instantly; Divine Light eternally embraces all now, drawing many into lasting, inner, nurturing, divine peace—always transforming, inspiring, lifting, mending, uniting, saving, illuminating, comforting, and liberating."

These songs draw from Scripture and are designed for use across all Christian traditions.

Our Vision

LeMisAvailable Music Production envisions a world restored to hope, unity, and compassion through the transformative power of music. We believe the world was not meant to be fragmented—it was originally created as one. Through music, we seek to remind humanity of this shared origin and to heal the divisions that separate us today.

Our vision is to inspire hearts, uplift spirits, and awaken a sense of belonging—especially among children and youth—so they may grow with hope, purpose, and confidence for the future.

Our Purpose

The purpose of LeMisAvailable Music Production is to create and share music that speaks to the soul, brings light into darkness, and offers hope to those who feel lost, broken, or discouraged. We are committed to encouraging children and young people by nurturing their creativity, affirming their worth, and guiding them toward a hopeful future through meaningful musical experiences.

Through artistic excellence and heartfelt expression, we strive to comfort those struggling with depression, loneliness, or despair—reminding them that they are seen, valued, and never alone. Our music exists to heal, to connect, and to restore hope—one voice, one heart, one world at a time.
