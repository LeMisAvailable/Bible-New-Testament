Treasure in Jars of Clay

2 Corinthians Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

We are fragile vessels, broken and worn,
Yet inside us treasure, a light newly born,
The glory of God shines through our cracks,
His power made perfect in what we lack.
Pressed on every side but not crushed down,
Perplexed but never lost, still standing ground.

[Chorus]

We carry this treasure in jars of clay,
So all can see His power on display,
Struck down but not destroyed, we rise again,
The light of Christ shining through our pain,
Treasure in jars of clay, His glory we proclaim.

[Verse 2]

We do not lose heart though outwardly we fade,
Inwardly renewed with each passing day,
Our light afflictions, momentary here,
Are working eternal glory, crystal clear.
We fix our eyes on things we cannot see,
For what is seen is temporary.

[Chorus]

We carry this treasure in jars of clay,
So all can see His power on display,
Struck down but not destroyed, we rise again,
The light of Christ shining through our pain,
Treasure in jars of clay, His glory we proclaim.

[Bridge]

God who said let light shine out of dark,
Has made His light shine within our heart,
The knowledge of His glory in Christ's face,
Illuminates our souls with endless grace.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
