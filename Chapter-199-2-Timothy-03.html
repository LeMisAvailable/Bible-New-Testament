All Scripture Breathed

2 Timothy Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In the last days, difficult times will come,
People will be lovers of self, struck dumb,
Proud and arrogant, disobedient hearts,
Unholy and heartless, tearing apart.
Having the appearance of godly ways,
But denying its power through all their days.

[Chorus]

All Scripture is breathed out by God above,
Profitable for teaching, reproof, and love,
For correction and training in righteousness so true,
That the man of God may be complete in all he'll do.

[Verse 2]

You have followed my teaching and my way,
My aim in life through each and every day,
My faith and patience, love that never ends,
Persecutions suffered—the Lord defends.
From childhood you have known the sacred Word,
The wisest, truest teaching ever heard.

[Chorus]

All Scripture is breathed out by God above,
Profitable for teaching, reproof, and love,
For correction and training in righteousness so true,
That the man of God may be complete in all he'll do.

[Bridge]

All who desire to live godly in Christ,
Will be persecuted, but pay the price,
Continue in what you have learned and believed,
From those you trusted, the truth received.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
