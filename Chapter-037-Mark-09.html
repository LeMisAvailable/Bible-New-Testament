The Transfiguration

Mark Chapter 9

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Majestic Worship

[Verse 1]

Upon the mountain, high and bright,
His garments shining, dazzling white,
Moses and Elijah standing there,
Glory filling all the air.
Peter speaks of tents to build,
Not knowing what these moments willed.

[Chorus]

This is My beloved Son,
Listen to Him, the chosen One,
On the mountain, glory shines,
Heaven and earth in love entwined,
This is My beloved Son.

[Verse 2]

A father brings his suffering child,
By evil spirits driven wild,
"If You can do anything," he pleads,
"Lord, help my unbelief," he needs.
Jesus speaks with power true,
The demon flees—the boy made new.

[Chorus]

This is My beloved Son,
Listen to Him, the chosen One,
On the mountain, glory shines,
Heaven and earth in love entwined,
This is My beloved Son.

[Bridge]

The greatest must become the least,
A servant's heart, from first to last,
Whoever welcomes one small child,
Welcomes Me with love so mild.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
