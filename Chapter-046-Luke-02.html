Born in Bethlehem

Luke Chapter 2 - Birth of Jesus, Shepherds, and Temple

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

No room at the inn, just a manger bed,
Where Mary laid her baby's head,
The King of Glory, wrapped in rags,
Heaven's treasure in swaddling bands.
A census brought them to this place,
Where God revealed His saving grace.

[Chorus]

Glory in the highest, peace on earth,
Angels sing of the Savior's birth,
Shepherds run to see the holy sight,
The Light of the World born this night.

[Verse 2]

On Judean hills, the shepherds kept their sheep,
When glory broke through midnight's sleep,
"Fear not," the angel's voice rang clear,
"Good news of joy for all is here."
They ran to find what they'd been told,
A baby worth more than all the gold.

[Chorus]

Glory in the highest, peace on earth,
Angels sing of the Savior's birth,
Shepherds run to see the holy sight,
The Light of the World born this night.

[Bridge]

Simeon held the promise in his arms,
Anna praised through all her years of psalms,
In the temple courts, the truth revealed,
Salvation's plan at last unsealed.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
