Watch and Pray

Luke Chapter 21

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

A widow gave her final coins,
Two copper pieces, all she owns,
The rich gave much from overflow,
But she gave all, though no one knows.
"This temple grand will fall one day,
Not one stone left," He dared to say.

[Chorus]

Watch and pray, the time draws near,
Stand firm in faith and cast out fear,
Heaven and earth will pass away,
But My words forever stay.
Lift your heads, redemption's close,
The Son of Man—the hope of those who chose.

[Verse 2]

Wars and rumors, nations rise,
Earthquakes shake beneath the skies,
Famines, plagues, and signs above,
But do not fear—hold to His love.
They'll arrest you, drag you down,
But wisdom will be your crown.

[Chorus]

Watch and pray, the time draws near,
Stand firm in faith and cast out fear,
Heaven and earth will pass away,
But My words forever stay.
Lift your heads, redemption's close,
The Son of Man—the hope of those who chose.

[Bridge]

Jerusalem surrounded, tread,
By Gentile feet until time's end,
The sun and moon will lose their light,
The powers shaken in the night.
But when the fig tree shows its leaves,
Summer's near for those who believe.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
