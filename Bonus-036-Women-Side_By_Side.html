Side By Side

Priscilla and Aquila - Partners in Ministry - Acts 18

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Upbeat Contemporary

[Verse 1]

A husband and wife team making tents by day,
When Paul came to Corinth, they showed him the way,
Working together in business and in faith,
Their home became a church, a holy place.
Priscilla and Aquila, side by side,
In ministry together, nothing to hide.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

When Apollos spoke boldly but didn't fully know,
They took him aside, helped his knowledge grow,
Explained the way of God more perfectly,
A couple teaching with humility.
They risked their lives for Paul's sake, the Scripture says,
All the churches thanked them for all their days.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Two are better than one, the Scripture's true,
When one falls down the other helps them through,
In marriage and in ministry combined,
A partnership that glorifies the Divine.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
