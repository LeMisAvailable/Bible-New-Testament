The Truth Will Set You Free

John Chapter 8

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

They brought a woman caught in sin,
Stones ready, judgment closing in,
"Let him without sin cast the first,"
One by one, convicted, they dispersed.
"Neither do I condemn," He said,
"Go and sin no more instead."

[Chorus]

I am the Light of the world,
Walk with Me, let truth unfurl,
If you hold to what I say,
The truth will set you free today.

[Verse 2]

Slaves to sin, we cannot see,
But the Son can set us free,
"Before Abraham was, I AM,"
The eternal, holy Lamb.
They picked up stones to take His life,
But He passed through their bitter strife.

[Chorus]

I am the Light of the world,
Walk with Me, let truth unfurl,
If you hold to what I say,
The truth will set you free today.

[Bridge]

I speak what I have seen above,
The Father's heart, the Father's love,
When you lift the Son of Man,
Then you'll know just who I am.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
