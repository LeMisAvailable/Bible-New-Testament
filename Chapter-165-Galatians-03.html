Children of Promise

Galatians Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

O foolish Galatians, who cast the spell,
That drew you from grace where you once did dwell?
Before your eyes Christ was crucified,
Did the Spirit come when the law was tried?
Abraham believed and it was his gain,
Righteousness through faith, not works in vain.

[Chorus]

We are children of the promise made,
Heirs of Abraham through faith displayed,
No more slave or free, no woman or man,
All are one in Christ, God's eternal plan.

[Verse 2]

The law was our guardian until Christ came,
To lead us to faith in His holy name,
Cursed is everyone who hangs on a tree,
Christ became the curse to set us free.
The promise to Abraham's seed was given,
Christ the seed, the door to heaven.

[Chorus]

We are children of the promise made,
Heirs of Abraham through faith displayed,
No more slave or free, no woman or man,
All are one in Christ, God's eternal plan.

[Bridge]

Clothed in Christ through baptism's wave,
No distinction from the cradle to the grave,
If you belong to Christ, then Abraham's seed,
Heirs according to promise, freed indeed.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
