The Jerusalem Council

Acts Chapter 15

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In Jerusalem the elders gathered round,
Seeking wisdom, seeking holy ground,
What must Gentiles do to find their way?
Must they bear the law from yesterday?
Peter rose and spoke of grace poured out,
God knows every heart without a doubt.

[Chorus]

By grace alone we come before the throne,
No burden heavy, no heart left unknown,
The Spirit speaks, the council hears the call,
One faith, one love, one Savior for us all.

[Verse 2]

James stood tall with wisdom from above,
Declaring freedom born of endless love,
No yoke too heavy shall we place on them,
For God rebuilds the fallen tent again.
They sent the letter with a joyful heart,
United now though miles may keep apart.

[Chorus]

By grace alone we come before the throne,
No burden heavy, no heart left unknown,
The Spirit speaks, the council hears the call,
One faith, one love, one Savior for us all.

[Bridge]

Paul and Barnabas must walk separate ways,
Yet both would serve through all their days,
Sometimes hearts divide but missions grow,
God uses every path we choose to go.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
