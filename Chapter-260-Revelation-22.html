River of Life, Jesus is Coming

Revelation Chapter 22

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Joyful Anticipation Anthem

[Verse 1]

A river flows from the throne of God,
Crystal clear through streets the saints have trod,
The tree of life on either side does grow,
Twelve fruits for healing, endless overflow.
No curse shall ever touch this holy place,
His servants see Him, glory face to face.

[Chorus]

Come, Lord Jesus, come quickly now,
Before Your throne we humbly bow,
The Spirit and the Bride say "Come!"
Let all who thirst drink, Kingdom come!

[Verse 2]

No need for lamp, no sun to light the way,
The Lord God shines in everlasting day,
They shall reign forever, world without end,
The Morning Star, our Savior and our friend.
"Behold, I'm coming soon!" the Lord declares,
Blessed are all who heed and who prepare.

[Chorus]

Come, Lord Jesus, come quickly now,
Before Your throne we humbly bow,
The Spirit and the Bride say "Come!"
Let all who thirst drink, Kingdom come!

[Bridge]

I am the Alpha and Omega bright,
The First and Last, the Morning Light,
Let all who hear repeat this call,
The grace of Jesus be with all.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
