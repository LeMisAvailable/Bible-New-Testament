The King Has Come

John Chapter 12

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Mary poured perfume so rare,
The fragrance filled the evening air,
Anointing Him for burial's day,
A gift of love along the way.
Palm branches waved, hosannas cried,
"Blessed is the King!" the crowds replied.

[Chorus]

Hosanna! The King rides in,
Humble on a donkey, free from sin,
The hour has come for glory's call,
When lifted up, He'll draw us all.

[Verse 2]

Greeks came seeking Jesus' face,
Longing to behold His grace,
"Unless a grain of wheat shall die,
It stands alone," He did reply.
"But if it dies, much fruit it bears,"
The Father's voice broke through the prayers.

[Chorus]

Hosanna! The King rides in,
Humble on a donkey, free from sin,
The hour has come for glory's call,
When lifted up, He'll draw us all.

[Bridge]

Walk while you have the light,
Believe before the night,
Though many saw and would not trust,
In Jesus alone our hope is just.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
