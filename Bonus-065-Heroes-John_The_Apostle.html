The One Jesus Loved

John - The Beloved Disciple - Gospel of John, 1-3 John, Revelation

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Intimate Worship

[Verse 1]

They called us "Sons of Thunder,"
My brother James and me,
We wanted thrones and power,
But love changed what we'd be.
As I walked with Jesus,
Something changed within my heart.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

I stood beneath the cross that day,
When all the others fled,
"Take care of My mother," Jesus said,
And I took her home instead.
I ran to the empty tomb,
And I believed before I understood.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

I'm the one Jesus loved,
I leaned upon His chest that night,
"God is love," I wrote for all the world,
And perfect love casts out all fear.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
