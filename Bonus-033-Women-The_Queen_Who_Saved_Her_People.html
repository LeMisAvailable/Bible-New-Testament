The Queen Who Saved Her People

Esther - Courage in Crisis - Book of Esther

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Dramatic Pop

[Verse 1]

An orphan girl who became a queen,
The most beautiful Persia had ever seen,
But no one knew the secret I carried inside,
My Jewish heritage I had to hide.
Then Haman's plot threatened to destroy,
Every Jewish man, woman, girl, and boy.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Mordecai said, "Perhaps for such a time as this,
You became queen—don't let fear dismiss."
"If I perish, I perish," I bravely declared,
Three days of fasting, then I was prepared.
I stood before the king, uninvited but bold,
The scepter was raised—my story unfolds.

Remember the lives you might save,

Your moment is coming—it's almost time.

[Outro]

Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
