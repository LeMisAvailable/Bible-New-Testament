The Pillar of Truth

1 Timothy Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

If anyone aspires to oversee,
He desires a noble task, as it should be,
Above reproach, faithful in marriage bond,
Sober-minded, self-controlled, and fond,
Of hospitality, able to teach,
Gentle and kind in all he'll preach.

[Chorus]

The church of the living God stands tall,
A pillar and foundation through it all,
The mystery of godliness revealed,
He appeared in flesh, by the Spirit sealed.

[Verse 2]

Deacons likewise must be dignified,
Not double-tongued, with nothing to hide,
Holding the mystery of faith sincere,
Managing their households without fear.
Those who serve well gain a standing true,
Great confidence in the faith shines through.

[Chorus]

The church of the living God stands tall,
A pillar and foundation through it all,
The mystery of godliness revealed,
He appeared in flesh, by the Spirit sealed.

[Bridge]

Seen by angels, proclaimed among the nations,
Believed on in the world, beyond all stations,
Taken up in glory, raised on high,
The truth of Christ will never, ever die.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
