Keep Yourselves in Love

Jude Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Jude, a servant of Jesus Christ,
To those called and loved, a sacrifice,
Contend for the faith once delivered to all,
Stand firm in the truth, never fall.
Certain men have crept in unseen,
Turning grace into something unclean.

[Chorus]

Keep yourselves in the love of God,
Wait for mercy on the path you've trod,
Build yourselves up in your holy faith,
Pray in the Spirit, standing in grace,
Keep yourselves in His embrace.

[Verse 2]

Remember Sodom and the angels who fell,
Examples of judgment, warnings to tell,
These dreamers pollute their own flesh,
Rejecting authority, failing the test.
They are clouds without rain, blown away,
Autumn trees twice dead, gone astray.

[Chorus]

Keep yourselves in the love of God,
Wait for mercy on the path you've trod,
Build yourselves up in your holy faith,
Pray in the Spirit, standing in grace,
Keep yourselves in His embrace.

[Bridge]

Be merciful to those who doubt,
Snatch others from the fire's shout,
To Him who keeps you from falling down,
And presents you blameless with joy unbound.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
