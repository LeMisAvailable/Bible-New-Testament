Bread of Life

John Chapter 6

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Five thousand souls on the mountainside,
With nothing to eat, nowhere to hide,
A boy with five loaves and two small fish,
Jesus took them, fulfilled every wish.
Twelve baskets full when all had been fed,
The multitude marveled at heaven's bread.

[Chorus]

I am the Bread of Life, come to Me,
Whoever believes will never hungry be,
I came down from heaven above,
To fill your soul with eternal love.

[Verse 2]

Walking on water through the storm,
"It is I, don't be alarmed,"
They sought Him out for earthly bread,
"Seek the food that lasts," He said.
Many turned back, the teaching too hard,
But Peter stayed close, forever on guard.

[Chorus]

I am the Bread of Life, come to Me,
Whoever believes will never hungry be,
I came down from heaven above,
To fill your soul with eternal love.

[Bridge]

"Lord, to whom shall we go?"
Peter cried with faith aglow,
"You have the words of eternal life,
We believe through every strife."

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
