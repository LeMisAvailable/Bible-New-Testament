Rights of an Apostle

1 Corinthians Chapter 9

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Am I not free? Am I not sent?
Have I not seen the Lord's descent?
You are my proof, my work in Christ,
The seal of labor sacrificed,
I have the right to eat and drink,
But for the gospel, I will shrink.

[Chorus]

I become all things to win,
To the weak, to those in sin,
I run the race to win the prize,
With fixed heart and focused eyes.

[Verse 2]

The soldier serves and eats his share,
The farmer reaps with rightful care,
But I forgo what I am owed,
To ease the gospel on the road,
Woe to me if I don't preach,
The saving grace within my reach.

[Chorus]

I become all things to win,
To the weak, to those in sin,
I run the race to win the prize,
With fixed heart and focused eyes.

[Bridge]

I discipline this mortal frame,
Lest after preaching, I bear shame,
Not aimlessly I run the track,
I press ahead, I don't look back.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
