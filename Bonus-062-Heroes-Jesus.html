The Name Above All Names

Jesus - The Son of God, Savior of the World - The Gospels

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Majestic Worship Anthem

[Verse 1]

Born in a manger, heaven's King,
Angels announced His birth with songs to sing,
He grew in wisdom, stature, and grace,
God wrapped in flesh with a human face.
He healed the sick, He raised the dead,
He walked on water, calmed the storm.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

They nailed Him to a wooden cross,
The sky went dark, it seemed all was lost,
"It is finished!" He cried that day,
He bore our sins, took them away.
On the third day He rose again,
Defeating death, defeating shame!

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

He is the way, the truth, the life,
No one comes to the Father except through Christ,
Lion of Judah, Lamb who was slain,
Morning Star, He calls us by name.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
