Set Your Mind Above

Colossians Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

If then you have been raised with Christ on high,
Seek the things above where He sits at right,
Set your mind on things above, not earthly ways,
For you have died, your life is hid always.
When Christ appears, your life, your everything,
You will appear with Him in glory's ring.

[Chorus]

Put on the new self, renewed in the image divine,
Where Christ is all, in all hearts He will shine,
Clothe yourselves with compassion, kindness, and grace,
Humility, gentleness in every place.

[Verse 2]

Put to death what's earthly: impurity and greed,
Sexual immorality, every evil deed,
Put away anger, wrath, and malice too,
Slander and lies, be honest through and through.
You've taken off the old with all its ways,
And put on the new that grows in righteous praise.

[Chorus]

Put on the new self, renewed in the image divine,
Where Christ is all, in all hearts He will shine,
Clothe yourselves with compassion, kindness, and grace,
Humility, gentleness in every place.

[Bridge]

Above all these put on love's perfect bond,
Let the peace of Christ in your heart respond,
Let the word of Christ dwell richly within,
Teaching and singing, glorifying Him.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
