Overcoming the World

1 John Chapter 5

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Everyone who believes that Jesus is the Christ,
Is born of God and loves the One who gave us life,
By this we know we love the children of the King,
When we love God and obey, to His commands we cling.
For this is love: to keep His commandments true,
His commands are not burdensome for me and you.

[Chorus]

This is the victory that overcomes the world,
Our faith like a banner unfurled,
Who is it that overcomes the darkness and the strife?
Only the one who believes Jesus is the life.

[Verse 2]

This is He who came by water and by blood,
Jesus Christ, not by water only, but the flood,
The Spirit testifies because the Spirit is true,
Three bear witness: Spirit, water, blood for you.
If we receive the testimony of mankind,
Greater is God's testimony, truth we find.

[Chorus]

This is the victory that overcomes the world,
Our faith like a banner unfurled,
Who is it that overcomes the darkness and the strife?
Only the one who believes Jesus is the life.

[Bridge]

God has given us eternal life so free,
This life is in His Son for you and me,
Whoever has the Son has life forevermore,
We know the true God, He is at the door.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
