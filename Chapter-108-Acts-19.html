Ephesus Ablaze

Acts Chapter 19

Scripture: Acts 19

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Intro]

We are chasing through the sacred Word,
Acts, chapter nineteen is heard,
Do not fear, the Lord has said,
In Ephesus the Spirit spread,
And magic books were burned instead.

[Verse 1]

The Spirit fell in Ephesus,
With tongues of fire victorious,
Great miracles through Paul were done,
The servant of the Holy One.
His cloths could heal the sick and lame,
And demons fled at Jesus' name.

[Chorus]

So burn the scrolls, let magic fade,
The name of Jesus is displayed,
No silver idol stands His might,
God's Word spreads like the morning light.

[Verse 2]

The sons of Sceva tried their hand,
But demons know who takes a stand,
"Jesus I know, and Paul," they cried,
"But who are you?" left terrified.
Fear fell on all and filled the air,
They burned their books in one great flare.

[Chorus]

So burn the scrolls, let magic fade,
The name of Jesus is displayed,
No silver idol stands His might,
God's Word spreads like the morning light.

[Bridge]

The silversmiths were stirred to rage,
The crowd roared loud like beasts in cage,
"Great is Artemis!" they sang so long,
But God's truth still remains so strong.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
I trust Your heart and all You've planned.

[Chorus]

Amen.

[Pause]

[Spoken Word, Deep Male Baritone, Calm, Reverent]
Darkness abates, vanquished instantly;
Divine Light eternally embraces all now;
Drawing many into lasting, inner, nurturing, divine peace;
Always transforming, inspiring, lifting, mending, uniting, saving, illuminating, comforting, and liberating.
