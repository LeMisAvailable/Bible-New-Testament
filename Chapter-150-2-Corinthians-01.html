God of All Comfort

2 Corinthians Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In the valley of shadows, I cried out Your name,
Broken and weary, consumed by the flame,
But You came like a whisper, so gentle, so near,
The Father of mercies wiping every tear.
In my deepest affliction, You held me so tight,
The God of all comfort, my refuge, my light.

[Chorus]

You are the God of all comfort and grace,
In every trial, I see Your face,
What You have given, I now give away,
Sharing Your mercy with hearts in dismay,
God of all comfort, with me You remain.

[Verse 2]

Paul knew the suffering, the weight and the pain,
Pressed beyond measure, yet never in vain,
For every wound carried becomes healing balm,
Every storm weathered leads others to calm.
We comfort each other with comfort received,
A chain of compassion for all who have grieved.

[Chorus]

You are the God of all comfort and grace,
In every trial, I see Your face,
What You have given, I now give away,
Sharing Your mercy with hearts in dismay,
God of all comfort, with me You remain.

[Bridge]

Through Asia's darkness, death seemed so sure,
But God who raises made our hope pure,
He delivered, delivers, will deliver again,
Our faithful Savior, now and to the end.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
