The Greatest Commandment

Mark Chapter 12

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Soulful Gospel

[Verse 1]

They tried to trap Him with their words,
But wisdom like they'd never heard,
"Render unto Caesar what is his,
To God what belongs in heavenly bliss."
A scribe asked, "What's the greatest command?"
And Jesus made them understand.

[Chorus]

Love the Lord your God with all your heart,
With all your soul, with every part,
Love your neighbor as yourself each day,
No greater commandment shows the way,
Love the Lord your God with all your heart.

[Verse 2]

The widow came with coins so small,
Two mites—she gave her very all,
While rich men gave from overflow,
Her sacrifice made heaven glow.
Jesus said, "She gave much more,
Her faith unlocked heaven's door."

[Chorus]

Love the Lord your God with all your heart,
With all your soul, with every part,
Love your neighbor as yourself each day,
No greater commandment shows the way,
Love the Lord your God with all your heart.

[Bridge]

The stone the builders cast aside,
Became the cornerstone of life,
The Lord has done this wondrous thing,
To Him alone our praises sing.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
