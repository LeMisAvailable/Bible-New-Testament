Jesus Greater than Moses

Hebrews Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Rock Ballad

[Verse 1]

Holy brothers, sharing heaven's call,
Fix your eyes on Jesus, Lord of all,
Faithful to the One who appointed Him,
Just as Moses served with faith within.
But the builder has more honor than the house,
Jesus built all things, His glory aroused.

[Chorus]

Greater than Moses, the Son over all,
Don't harden your hearts when you hear His call,
Today if you hear Him, don't turn away,
His house we are, if we hold faith today.

[Verse 2]

In the wilderness they tested God,
Forty years they wandered, hearts so hard,
They saw His works yet still they went astray,
Their unbelief kept rest at bay.
So I swore in anger, God declared,
"They shall not enter rest I had prepared."

[Chorus]

Greater than Moses, the Son over all,
Don't harden your hearts when you hear His call,
Today if you hear Him, don't turn away,
His house we are, if we hold faith today.

[Bridge]

Encourage each other while it's called today,
Let no sinful heart lead you astray,
We share in Christ if we hold firm and true,
The confidence we had when faith was new.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
