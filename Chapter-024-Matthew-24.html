Signs of the End

Matthew Chapter 24

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Epic Cinematic

[Verse 1]

"When will these things be?" the disciples asked,
Jesus spoke of futures unsurpassed,
Wars and rumors, nations will rise,
Earthquakes and famines beneath the skies.
False prophets coming with deceitful signs,
But the one who endures till the end—shines.

[Chorus]

Heaven and earth will pass away,
But My words will stand, they're here to stay,
No one knows the hour, not even the Son,
Keep watch and be ready—the time will come.

[Verse 2]

The sun will darken, the moon won't glow,
Stars will fall and the powers below,
Then the sign of the Son of Man appears,
Coming on clouds with angels near.
He'll gather His chosen from every end,
From the four winds, heaven will descend.

[Chorus]

Heaven and earth will pass away,
But My words will stand, they're here to stay,
No one knows the hour, not even the Son,
Keep watch and be ready—the time will come.

[Bridge]

Like the days of Noah, eating and drinking,
Unaware of the flood, never thinking,
Two in the field, one taken away,
Stay awake and faithful every day.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
