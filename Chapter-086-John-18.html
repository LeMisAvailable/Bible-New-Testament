The Arrest and Trial

John Chapter 18

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

In the garden, torches blazed,
Judas led the soldiers, amazed,
"Who do you seek?" the Savior said,
"I am He," and they fell like dead.
Peter's sword flashed through the night,
But Jesus stopped the raging fight.

[Chorus]

Shall I not drink the cup He gave?
The Father's will, my soul to save,
Bound and led, yet still so free,
Love held Him there for you and me.

[Verse 2]

Peter followed from afar,
Warming by the courtyard star,
"You're not one of them?" they pressed,
Three times denied, he failed the test.
The rooster crowed, his heart was torn,
The prophecy fulfilled that morn.

[Chorus]

Shall I not drink the cup He gave?
The Father's will, my soul to save,
Bound and led, yet still so free,
Love held Him there for you and me.

[Bridge]

"My kingdom is not of this world,"
Before Pilate, truth unfurled,
"What is truth?" the governor asked,
Standing before Truth unmasked.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
