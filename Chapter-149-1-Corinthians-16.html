Final Greetings

1 Corinthians Chapter 16

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

On the first day set aside,
A collection for the saints with pride,
Giving as the Lord has blessed,
Generosity confessed,
Each one sharing in the call,
To love and care for one and all.

[Chorus]

Be on your guard, stand firm in faith,
Be courageous, strong in grace,
Do everything in love divine,
Let Christ's own heart in your heart shine.

[Verse 2]

A door has opened wide for me,
Though many stand as enemies,
I'll stay to work until it's done,
Fighting battles to be won,
Greet the brothers, greet with care,
Holy kisses everywhere.

[Chorus]

Be on your guard, stand firm in faith,
Be courageous, strong in grace,
Do everything in love divine,
Let Christ's own heart in your heart shine.

[Bridge]

Maranatha—come, O Lord,
We wait upon Your faithful word,
The grace of Jesus be with you,
My love in Christ is always true.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
