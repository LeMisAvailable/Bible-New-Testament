From Simon To Peter

Peter - The Rock - The Gospels, Acts

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Powerful Rock

[Verse 1]

I was casting nets when He called my name,
"Follow Me, I'll make you fish for men,"
I dropped everything and followed Him,
Simon the fisherman, born again.
I walked on water, then I sank,
But Jesus never gave up on me.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

I denied Him three times that awful night,
Before the rooster crowed,
Jesus looked at me, and I wept bitterly,
The weight of my failure showed.
But on the shore He cooked me breakfast,
"Do you love Me?" three times He asked.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

From Simon to Peter, He changed my name,
From sand to rock, I'd never be the same,
He forgave my shame,
I'm not who I was, I'm who He says I am.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
