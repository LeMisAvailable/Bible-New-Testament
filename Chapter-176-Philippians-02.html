The Mind of Christ

Philippians Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

If there's encouragement in Christ our Lord,
Comfort from love, fellowship adored,
Make my joy complete, be of one mind,
In humility, consider others' kind.
Do nothing from selfish ambition's plea,
Look to others' interests, not just what you see.

[Chorus]

Have this mind among you that was in Christ,
Who being God, did not grasp at the highest,
Emptied Himself, took the servant's form,
Humbled to death, through the darkest storm.

[Verse 2]

He became obedient unto death,
Even the cross with His final breath,
Therefore God exalted Him on high,
A name above all names that never dies.
Every knee shall bow, every tongue confess,
Jesus Christ is Lord, forever blessed.

[Chorus]

Have this mind among you that was in Christ,
Who being God, did not grasp at the highest,
Emptied Himself, took the servant's form,
Humbled to death, through the darkest storm.

[Bridge]

Work out your salvation with fear and awe,
For God works in you, His perfect law,
Shine like stars in a crooked age,
Holding the word of life on every page.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
