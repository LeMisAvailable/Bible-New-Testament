The Cornerstone

Luke Chapter 20

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

"By what authority?" they cried,
But Jesus knew their hearts had lied,
John's baptism—heaven or man?
They feared the crowd and had no plan.
So Jesus spoke a parable,
A vineyard, tenants, judgment fell.

[Chorus]

The stone the builders threw away,
Has become the cornerstone today,
Whoever falls upon this rock,
Will be broken, but those who mock—
On whom it falls, they'll be crushed to dust,
In Christ alone we place our trust.

[Verse 2]

They sent their servants one by one,
Each beaten, shamed before the Son,
"Perhaps my beloved they'll respect,"
But they killed Him without regret.
The vineyard given to others now,
To those who bear fruit and will bow.

[Chorus]

The stone the builders threw away,
Has become the cornerstone today,
Whoever falls upon this rock,
Will be broken, but those who mock—
On whom it falls, they'll be crushed to dust,
In Christ alone we place our trust.

[Bridge]

Give to Caesar what is his,
Give to God the life you live,
He's not God of the dead but living,
All are alive to Him who's giving.
David's Lord, yet David's Son,
The mystery of the Holy One.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
