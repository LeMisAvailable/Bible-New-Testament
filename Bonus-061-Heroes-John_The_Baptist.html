Voice In The Wilderness

John the Baptist - The Forerunner - Matthew 3, Luke 1

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Raw Folk Rock

[Verse 1]

I was filled with the Spirit from my mother's womb,
I leaped when Mary walked in the room,
Set apart before I was even born,
To announce the coming of the Holy One.
I grew up in the desert, wild and free,
Just locusts, honey, and a prophet's call.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

"Repent! The kingdom of heaven is near!"
Then one day Jesus Himself appeared,
"I should be baptized by You!" I said,
The heavens opened, the Spirit came down.
"Behold the Lamb who takes away all sin!"
My mission was complete, His would begin.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

I'm the voice in the wilderness crying out,
Prepare the way of the Lord!
He must increase, I must decrease,
That's the joy of the forerunner's call.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
