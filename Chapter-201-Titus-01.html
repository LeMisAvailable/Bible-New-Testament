Appointed for the Faith

Titus Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Paul, a servant of God, apostle true,
For the faith of the elect I write to you,
Knowledge of truth that leads to godly ways,
Hope of eternal life through endless days.
God who never lies promised long ago,
At the proper time, His Word He'd show.

[Chorus]

Appoint elders in every town today,
Above reproach in every single way,
Holding firm to the trustworthy Word,
Teaching sound doctrine that must be heard.

[Verse 2]

To Titus, my true child in common faith,
Grace and peace from God—His loving embrace,
I left you in Crete to set things straight,
To put in order what could not wait.
An overseer must be blameless and sound,
Not arrogant or quick-tempered, but profound.

[Chorus]

Appoint elders in every town today,
Above reproach in every single way,
Holding firm to the trustworthy Word,
Teaching sound doctrine that must be heard.

[Bridge]

Many are insubordinate, empty talk,
Deceivers who must be silenced on their walk,
Rebuke them sharply that they may be sound,
In faith and truth, let purity abound.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
