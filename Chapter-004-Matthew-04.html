Tested in the Desert

Matthew Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Intense Dramatic

[Verse 1]

Forty days in the wilderness alone,
Hungry and weary, flesh down to bone,
The tempter came with whispers sly,
"If You're the Son of God, don't deny—
Turn these stones to bread, ease Your pain,"
But Jesus answered, "Man shall not live in vain."

[Chorus]

It is written, it is done,
The Word of God defeats the evil one,
Every temptation meets its end,
When truth becomes our greatest friend.

[Verse 2]

On the temple's peak, the devil tried again,
"Throw Yourself down, let angels defend,"
Jesus stood firm, unmoved by pride,
"Do not test the Lord," He replied.
Then kingdoms offered, power and fame,
"Bow to me"—but Jesus overcame.

[Chorus]

It is written, it is done,
The Word of God defeats the evil one,
Every temptation meets its end,
When truth becomes our greatest friend.

[Bridge]

"Follow Me," He called by Galilee's shore,
Fishermen left their nets for something more,
Peter, Andrew, James, and John,
Dropped everything to carry on.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
