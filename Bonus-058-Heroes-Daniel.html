Dare To Stand Alone

Daniel - Faithful in Babylon - Book of Daniel

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Bold Pop Rock

[Verse 1]

They took me from Jerusalem,
When I was just a boy,
They changed my name, they changed my food,
Tried to steal my joy.
But I resolved within my heart,
I would not defile myself.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

When they made a law against my prayers,
I opened my window just the same,
Three times a day toward Jerusalem,
I called upon God's name.
They threw me to the lions,
But God shut their mouths that night.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Dare to stand alone,
When everyone else bows down,
God will be with you then,
He is always with us!

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
