[Intro]

Courage builds through the sacred Word,
Gospel of Matthew, chapter twenty-seven is heard,
Gethsemane heard Jesus pray,
The cross awaits this solemn day,
Our Savior bears our sins away.

[Verse 1]

Before Pilate Jesus then stood,
The crowd chose wrong misunderstood,
Crucify Him the voices cried,
Pilate washed his hands stepped aside.
They mocked Him with a thorny crown,
The King of Jews was beaten down.

[Chorus]

At Golgotha the sky turned dark,
And three hours of night left their mark,
My God why have You left Me here,
The greatest love would now appear.

[Verse 2]

Simon carried cross up high,
They nailed Him there beneath the sky,
Between two thieves the Savior hung,
While mockery flowed from every tongue.
Save Yourself if You're the One,
But He stayed for what must be done.

[Chorus]

At Golgotha the sky turned dark,
And three hours of night left their mark,
My God why have You left Me here,
The greatest love would now appear.

[Bridge]

The temple curtain tore in two,
The earth shook and they finally knew,
Surely this was the Son of God,
They laid Him where no man had trod.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
I trust Your heart and all You've planned.

[Chorus]

Amen.

[Pause]
