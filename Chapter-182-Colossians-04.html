Walk in Wisdom

Colossians Chapter 4

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Masters, treat your servants justly and fair,
Knowing you have a Master who's always there,
Continue steadfast in prayer, watch and give thanks,
Praying that God may open doors through the ranks.
That I may declare the mystery of Christ,
For which I am in chains, paying the price.

[Chorus]

Walk in wisdom toward outsiders each day,
Make the best of every moment along the way,
Let your speech be gracious, seasoned with salt,
That you may know how to answer without fault.

[Verse 2]

Tychicus will tell you all about me,
A beloved brother, faithful and free,
Onesimus, the faithful and dear,
They will tell you everything happening here.
Aristarchus sends his greetings in chains,
Mark and Jesus called Justus remain.

[Chorus]

Walk in wisdom toward outsiders each day,
Make the best of every moment along the way,
Let your speech be gracious, seasoned with salt,
That you may know how to answer without fault.

[Bridge]

Epaphras wrestles in prayer for you all,
That you stand mature, answering God's call,
Luke the beloved physician sends his love,
Grace be with you from the Father above.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
