Teach Us to Pray

Luke Chapter 11 - The Lord's Prayer and Woes

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

"Lord, teach us how to pray," they said,
And Jesus bowed His holy head,
"Our Father, hallowed be Your name,
Your kingdom come, on earth the same."
Give us bread for every day,
Forgive our debts as we obey.

[Chorus]

Ask and it will be given to you,
Seek and you will find what's true,
Knock and the door will open wide,
Your Father gives good gifts inside.

[Verse 2]

Woe to those who load men down,
With burdens heavy, wearing frowns,
You clean the outside of the cup,
But inside, greed fills you up.
The sign of Jonah points the way,
Someone greater stands here today.

[Chorus]

Ask and it will be given to you,
Seek and you will find what's true,
Knock and the door will open wide,
Your Father gives good gifts inside.

[Bridge]

A friend at midnight knocks for bread,
Persistence brings what must be said,
If earthly fathers know to give,
How much more will our Father live?

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
