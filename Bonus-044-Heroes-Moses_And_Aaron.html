Brothers On A Mission

Moses and Aaron - The Deliverer and His Voice - Exodus

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Epic Rock Gospel

[Verse 1]

I was a fugitive in the desert,
When God spoke from the flame,
"Go back to Egypt, set them free,"
But I was full of shame.
"Who am I to face a king?
I can barely speak!"

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

"Let my people go!" we cried,
Through plagues of blood and frogs,
We lifted up our staff and hands,
And watched the waters part.
Led two million through the sea,
With one united heart.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Brothers on a mission,
Standing before the throne,
Neither one alone,
God gives us partners for the race.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
