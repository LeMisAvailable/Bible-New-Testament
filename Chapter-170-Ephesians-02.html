Alive in Christ

Ephesians Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

We were dead in our trespasses and sin,
Walking in darkness, lost within,
Following the prince of the air,
Children of wrath beyond repair.
But God, who is rich in mercy's flow,
Made us alive, His love to show.

[Chorus]

By grace you have been saved through faith,
Not of yourselves, this gift of grace,
Not by works, so none can boast,
His workmanship, what matters most.

[Verse 2]

Once we were far, but now brought near,
By the blood of Christ, so precious, dear,
He broke the wall of hostility,
Creating one new humanity.
Through the cross He reconciled,
Jew and Gentile, reconciled.

[Chorus]

By grace you have been saved through faith,
Not of yourselves, this gift of grace,
Not by works, so none can boast,
His workmanship, what matters most.

[Bridge]

No longer strangers, aliens no more,
Fellow citizens at heaven's door,
Built on apostles and prophets true,
Christ the cornerstone, all things made new.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
