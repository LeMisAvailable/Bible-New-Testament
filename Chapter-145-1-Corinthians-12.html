One Body, Many Gifts

1 Corinthians Chapter 12

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

There are different gifts but one Spirit gives,
Different works but one Lord in whom we live,
To one is given wisdom's word,
To another faith that's never blurred,
Healing, miracles, prophecy too,
All the Spirit working through and through.

[Chorus]

We are one body, many parts designed,
The eye cannot say to the hand, "You're not mine,"
If one part suffers, we all feel the pain,
If one is honored, we share in the gain.

[Verse 2]

The foot cannot say, "I don't belong,"
Each part of the body makes it strong,
The weaker members seem to be,
The most important, can't you see?
God composed the body with such care,
That every part would find its share.

[Chorus]

We are one body, many parts designed,
The eye cannot say to the hand, "You're not mine,"
If one part suffers, we all feel the pain,
If one is honored, we share in the gain.

[Bridge]

Now eagerly desire the greater gifts,
But there's a way that higher lifts,
A more excellent road to take,
The path of love for Jesus' sake.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
