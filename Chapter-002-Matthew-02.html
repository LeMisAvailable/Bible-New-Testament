The Star and the Sword

Matthew Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Dramatic Orchestral

[Verse 1]

Wise men traveled from eastern lands,
Following light they could not understand,
A star was blazing, leading the way,
To find the King born on that day.
With gold and incense, myrrh in their hands,
They knelt before Him, fulfilling God's plans.

[Chorus]

A star shines bright in the darkest night,
Guiding the lost to the holy light,
Though tyrants rage and the wicked conspire,
Heaven protects what the world desires.

[Verse 2]

Herod trembled upon his throne,
A rival King—he would not be outshone,
With murder planned for innocent lives,
Darkness descended where evil thrives.
But God spoke gently through dreams once more,
"Rise up and flee to a distant shore."

[Chorus]

A star shines bright in the darkest night,
Guiding the lost to the holy light,
Though tyrants rage and the wicked conspire,
Heaven protects what the world desires.

[Bridge]

Rachel weeps for her children gone,
Yet through the sorrow, hope carries on,
From Egypt's refuge to Nazareth's hills,
The Father's purpose He still fulfills.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
