For God So Loved

John Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Nicodemus came by night,
Seeking truth, searching for light,
"Rabbi, we know You come from God,"
But the path seemed strange and odd.
"You must be born again," Christ said,
"Of water and Spirit, newly led."

[Chorus]

For God so loved this broken world,
He gave His Son, salvation unfurled,
Whoever believes shall never die,
But have eternal life on high.

[Verse 2]

Like the serpent raised up high,
The Son of Man must be lifted to the sky,
Not to condemn but to save,
To rescue souls from the grave.
Light has come into the night,
Will you step into the light?

[Chorus]

For God so loved this broken world,
He gave His Son, salvation unfurled,
Whoever believes shall never die,
But have eternal life on high.

[Bridge]

He must increase, I must decrease,
John declared with humble peace,
The Bridegroom's voice brings joy complete,
Heaven's love makes life so sweet.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
