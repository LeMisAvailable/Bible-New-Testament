Wisdom from the Spirit

1 Corinthians Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

I came to you not with lofty speech,
Not human wisdom I would teach,
In weakness, fear, and trembling hands,
I preached the cross—God's saving plan,
That your faith would not rest in men,
But in God's power—say it again.

[Chorus]

Eye has not seen, ear has not heard,
The depths of love beyond all words,
What God prepared for those who love,
Revealed by Spirit from above.

[Verse 2]

The rulers of this age don't know,
The hidden wisdom God would show,
They crucified the Lord of light,
Not knowing glory in their sight,
But we have received the Spirit's breath,
To understand His life from death.

[Chorus]

Eye has not seen, ear has not heard,
The depths of love beyond all words,
What God prepared for those who love,
Revealed by Spirit from above.

[Bridge]

The natural man cannot perceive,
What only Spirit-filled believe,
We have the mind of Christ our King,
His thoughts become our offering.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
