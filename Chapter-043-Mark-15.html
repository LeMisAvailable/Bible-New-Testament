The Crucifixion

Mark Chapter 15

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Mournful Worship

[Verse 1]

Before Pilate, the King stood bound,
"Crucify Him!" was the crowd's resound,
They chose Barabbas, set him free,
While Jesus walked to Calvary.
A crown of thorns upon His brow,
The Lamb of God fulfilling vow.

[Chorus]

Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani,
"My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?"
The veil was torn from top to ground,
In death, salvation has been found,
Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani.

[Verse 2]

At Golgotha, between two thieves,
He hung in pain while heaven grieves,
Darkness covered all the land,
From noon till three by God's own hand.
With His final breath, He cried aloud,
And yielded up before the crowd.

[Chorus]

Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani,
"My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?"
The veil was torn from top to ground,
In death, salvation has been found,
Eloi, Eloi, lama sabachthani.

[Bridge]

The centurion stood and watched Him die,
"Truly, this was God's Son," his cry,
Joseph took the body down with care,
And laid Him in a tomb with prayer.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
