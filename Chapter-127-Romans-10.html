Confess and Believe

Romans Chapter 10

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

My heart's desire and prayer ascends,
That Israel's blindness finally ends,
They have zeal but lack the light,
Seeking their own righteous might.
Christ is the end of law for all,
Who believe and heed His call.

[Chorus]

If you confess with your mouth the Lord,
Believe in your heart His risen word,
You will be saved, the promise stands,
For everyone who takes His hands.

[Verse 2]

The word is near, upon your lips,
Within your heart, where faith equips,
No difference between Jew and Greek,
The same Lord blesses all who seek.
Everyone who calls His name,
Will be saved from sin and shame.

[Chorus]

If you confess with your mouth the Lord,
Believe in your heart His risen word,
You will be saved, the promise stands,
For everyone who takes His hands.

[Bridge]

How beautiful the feet that bring,
Good news of peace, of Christ the King,
Faith comes from hearing the word of God,
Across the earth where prophets trod.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
