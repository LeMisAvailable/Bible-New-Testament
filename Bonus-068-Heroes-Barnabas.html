Son Of Encouragement

Barnabas - The Encourager - Acts 4, 9, 11-15

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Uplifting Pop Gospel

[Verse 1]

I had a field, I sold it all,
Laid the money at the apostles' feet,
Because the church was my family,
And my brothers had needs to meet.
They called me Barnabas from that day on,
"Son of Encouragement," that's my name.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Paul was a convert no one trusted,
But I brought him to the apostles that day,
I saw his future when everyone else,
Was turning away.
I took John Mark, gave him a chance,
And Mark wrote a Gospel—that's no lie!

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

I'm the son of encouragement,
I believe in people when others don't,
I see potential when others won't,
Sometimes people need someone to believe.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
