Rise and Walk

John Chapter 5

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Thirty-eight years by the pool he lay,
Waiting for the waters to move his way,
Broken and hopeless, no one to help,
Trapped in a prison within himself.
Jesus saw him, knew his despair,
"Do you want to be well?" rang through the air.

[Chorus]

Rise, take up your mat and walk,
No more excuses, no more talk,
The Father works and so does the Son,
A life of wholeness has begun.

[Verse 2]

The Sabbath laws could not contain,
The One who breaks every chain,
"My Father is always at His work,"
No healing love will He ever shirk.
The dead will hear the voice of the Son,
And those who believe, their race is won.

[Chorus]

Rise, take up your mat and walk,
No more excuses, no more talk,
The Father works and so does the Son,
A life of wholeness has begun.

[Bridge]

The Scriptures testify of Me,
Moses wrote what you refuse to see,
I do not seek My glory alone,
The Father's will is all I've known.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
