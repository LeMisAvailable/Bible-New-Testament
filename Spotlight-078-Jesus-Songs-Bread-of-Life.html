Bread of Life

A Song of Spiritual Nourishment (John 6)

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Worship Anthem

[Intro]

Come and feast upon the bread divine,
Jesus offers living bread and wine,
No hunger here shall ever stay,
He satisfies us every day,
Let every follower now sing His praise.

[Verse 1]

He fed the thousands on the hill,
With loaves and fish, He ate their fill,
But greater food He came to give,
The bread of life by which we live,
Not manna falling from the sky,
But Jesus Christ who'll never die.

[Chorus]

Bread of Life, I come to You,
Hungry soul now fed anew,
You satisfy my deepest need,
On Your Word my spirit feeds.

[Verse 2]

"I am the bread," the Savior said,
"Come eat of Me and you'll be fed,
Whoever comes will never starve,
My body broken, flesh and heart,"
At His table we find rest,
With heaven's finest, we are blessed.

[Chorus]

Bread of Life, I come to You,
Hungry soul now fed anew,
You satisfy my deepest need,
On Your Word my spirit feeds.

[Bridge]

Take and eat, remember Me,
Broken bread for all to see,
In communion we partake,
Of the bread He chose to break.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
I trust Your heart and all You've planned.

[Chorus]

Amen.

[Pause]

[Spoken Word, Deep Male Baritone, Calm, Reverent]
Darkness abates, vanquished instantly;
Divine Light eternally embraces all now;
Drawing many into lasting, inner, nurturing, divine peace;
Always transforming, inspiring, lifting, mending, uniting, saving, illuminating, comforting, and liberating.
(Dramatic Orchestral, Cinematic, Church Choir Organ, Worship Anthem, Church Bells, Easy to sing along, Clear pronunciation, Devotional Ballad, Emotional, Orchestral, Reverent)
