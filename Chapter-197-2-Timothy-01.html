Fan Into Flame

2 Timothy Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

To Timothy, my beloved child in grace,
I thank God as I remember your face,
With a clear conscience, night and day I pray,
Longing to see you, wipe the tears away.
The sincere faith that dwelt first in your line,
From grandmother Lois, Eunice—now it's thine.

[Chorus]

Fan into flame the gift of God in you,
For He gave us not fear, but power to pursue,
Love and self-control, a spirit that is sound,
Do not be ashamed—let courage now abound.

[Verse 2]

Share in suffering for the Gospel's sake,
By the power of God, no chains can break,
He saved us with a holy, sacred call,
Not because of works but grace through all.
Before the ages, His purpose was revealed,
In Christ Jesus, our salvation sealed.

[Chorus]

Fan into flame the gift of God in you,
For He gave us not fear, but power to pursue,
Love and self-control, a spirit that is sound,
Do not be ashamed—let courage now abound.

[Bridge]

I know whom I have believed, He stands true,
He is able to guard what I've entrusted through,
Until that final day when all is known,
The deposit safe before His throne.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
