Let the Children Come

Matthew Chapter 19

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Warm Acoustic

[Verse 1]

They asked about divorce, testing His way,
Jesus pointed back to creation's first day,
"What God has joined, let no one divide,
Two become one, standing side by side."
Some things are hard, not all understand,
But kingdom purposes come from God's hand.

[Chorus]

Let the little children come to Me,
Don't hinder them—they're the key,
The kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these,
Come with childlike faith and be at ease.

[Verse 2]

A rich young man came seeking the way,
"What good must I do to have eternal day?"
"Keep the commands," Jesus replied,
"I've kept them all," the young man cried,
"Sell what you have, give to the poor,
Then follow Me"—but he walked out the door.

[Chorus]

Let the little children come to Me,
Don't hinder them—they're the key,
The kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these,
Come with childlike faith and be at ease.

[Bridge]

It's hard for the rich to enter in,
Easier for a camel—thread so thin,
"Then who can be saved?" they all replied,
"With God all things are possible," He testified.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
