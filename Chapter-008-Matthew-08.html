Lord of Wind and Wave

Matthew Chapter 8

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Epic Cinematic

[Verse 1]

A leper came and knelt before the Lord,
"If You are willing, heal me," he implored,
Jesus reached out and touched his shame,
"I am willing—be clean again."
A centurion's faith amazed them all,
"Just speak the word," was his humble call.

[Chorus]

Peace, be still, the Master speaks,
The wind obeys, the tempest sleeps,
What kind of man commands the sea?
The Son of God, who sets us free.

[Verse 2]

Peter's mother lay burning with fever's fire,
One touch from Jesus lifted her higher,
The evening came, the sick were brought,
And every healing that they sought,
He drove out demons with just a word,
The greatest Healer they'd ever heard.

[Chorus]

Peace, be still, the Master speaks,
The wind obeys, the tempest sleeps,
What kind of man commands the sea?
The Son of God, who sets us free.

[Bridge]

Foxes have holes, birds have their nests,
But the Son of Man had nowhere to rest,
"Follow Me," the cost was high,
Leave everything—let the dead bury the dead goodbye.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
