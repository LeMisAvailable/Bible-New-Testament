God Will Provide

Abraham and Isaac - Genesis 22

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Emotional Acoustic Ballad

[Verse 1]

I waited twenty-five years for this boy,
My promised son, my heart's greatest joy,
Now God is asking, "Will you give him back?"
My faith is tested, my heart might crack.
Three days we walked to Moriah's peak,
My son beside me, I could barely speak.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

"Father, I see the fire and wood,
But where's the lamb?" My boy understood
Something was wrong, tears in my eyes,
"God will provide," I said through my cries.
I raised the knife with trembling hand,
Then heard an angel's sweet command.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

A ram caught in the thicket there,
God provided, answered my prayer,
On the mountain of the Lord it's seen,
He provides for everything between.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
