Fire Fall Down

Elijah vs Prophets of Baal - 1 Kings 18

Duration: ~3 minutes | Style: Epic Rock Anthem

[Verse 1]

Four hundred fifty prophets on one side,
One man of God with nothing to hide,
"The god who answers by fire—He is God!"
Elijah stood where others never trod.
From morning to noon they danced and cried,
But Baal was silent—their god had lied.

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Verse 2]

Elijah rebuilt the altar stone by stone,
Dug a trench around it all alone,
"Pour water on it—do it again!"
Three times over, let the soaking begin.
Then he prayed to the God of Abraham,
"Show them today that You're the I AM!"

[Chorus]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.

[Bridge]

Fire fell down and consumed it all,
The stones, the water, at heaven's call,
The people cried, "The LORD is God!"
On Mount Carmel where Elijah trod.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
