The Angel and the Little Scroll

Revelation Chapter 10

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Ethereal Worship Ballad

[Verse 1]

A mighty angel descended, robed in clouds of white,
A rainbow crowned his head, his face was burning light,
His legs like pillars of fire, one foot on sea, one land,
A little scroll lay open in his mighty hand.
He shouted like a lion, seven thunders spoke,
But a voice said, "Seal it up, their words must not be broke."

[Chorus]

Sweet as honey on my lips, Your words I take inside,
Bitter in my stomach, but in You I will abide,
Mysteries unfolding as the ages turn to dust,
In Your sovereign timing, Lord, I place my trust.

[Verse 2]

The angel raised his hand toward heaven's throne above,
Swearing by the Living One, by justice and by love,
"There will be no more delay when the seventh sounds,
God's mystery complete, redemption shall abound."
John was told to take the scroll and eat it all within,
"Prophesy again," he heard, "let the word begin."

[Chorus]

Sweet as honey on my lips, Your words I take inside,
Bitter in my stomach, but in You I will abide,
Mysteries unfolding as the ages turn to dust,
In Your sovereign timing, Lord, I place my trust.

[Bridge]

To peoples, nations, languages, and kings,
The prophet must proclaim what judgment brings,
Though bitter truth may turn the soul to tears,
The sweetness of God's promise conquers fears.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Lord, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
