The Power of the Gospel

Romans Chapter 1

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Paul, a servant called apart,
With the gospel burning in his heart,
Good news promised long ago,
Through the prophets, now we know.
Jesus Christ, the risen Lord,
Son of God, by power restored.

[Chorus]

I am not ashamed of this gospel light,
It's the power of God to save tonight,
For the Jew and Gentile, all who believe,
By faith alone, we shall receive.

[Verse 2]

The wrath of God against the sin,
Of those who hold the truth within,
Creation shows His endless might,
Yet they turned from holy light.
Exchanging glory for a lie,
Worshipping the created sky.

[Chorus]

I am not ashamed of this gospel light,
It's the power of God to save tonight,
For the Jew and Gentile, all who believe,
By faith alone, we shall receive.

[Bridge]

Though the world descends to darkness deep,
A remnant true, the Lord will keep,
The righteous live by faith alone,
Through Christ we're brought before the throne.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
