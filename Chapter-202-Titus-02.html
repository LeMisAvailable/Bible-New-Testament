The Grace That Trains

Titus Chapter 2

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

Teach what accords with doctrine pure and sound,
Older men be sober, dignified, profound,
Sound in faith and love and steadfastness of soul,
Teaching younger men to keep themselves whole.
Older women reverent, training those who come,
To love their families, bringing glory from.

[Chorus]

For the grace of God has appeared to all,
Training us to answer heaven's call,
Renouncing ungodliness and worldly desire,
Living upright, godly, reaching higher.

[Verse 2]

Show yourself a model of good works each day,
In your teaching, integrity on display,
Dignity and sound speech none can condemn,
Adorning the doctrine of our God through them.
In everything, show the beauty of the faith,
Living as examples of His grace.

[Chorus]

For the grace of God has appeared to all,
Training us to answer heaven's call,
Renouncing ungodliness and worldly desire,
Living upright, godly, reaching higher.

[Bridge]

Waiting for our blessed hope to come,
The appearing of our God, the glorious One,
Who gave Himself to redeem us from all wrong,
A people zealous, to Him we belong.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
