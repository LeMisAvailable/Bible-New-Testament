Ministers of the New Covenant

2 Corinthians Chapter 3

Duration: ~2 minutes | Style: Devotional Ballad

[Verse 1]

You are a letter, written on hearts,
Not with ink but by Spirit's art,
Not on tablets of cold hard stone,
But living flesh where love is shown.
The old covenant brought death and fear,
But the Spirit gives life, drawing near.

[Chorus]

Where the Spirit of the Lord resides,
There is freedom, there He abides,
The veil is lifted, we see His face,
Transformed by glory, changed by grace,
From glory to glory, in His embrace.

[Verse 2]

Moses' face shone with fading light,
A glory destined to fade from sight,
But the ministry of the Spirit brings,
An everlasting glory that forever sings.
The letter kills but the Spirit gives life,
Ending our bondage, ending our strife.

[Chorus]

Where the Spirit of the Lord resides,
There is freedom, there He abides,
The veil is lifted, we see His face,
Transformed by glory, changed by grace,
From glory to glory, in His embrace.

[Bridge]

With unveiled faces we behold,
The Lord's own glory, more precious than gold,
We are being transformed day by day,
Into His image, come what may.

[Outro]

When fearful thoughts are intertwined,
You calm the storm inside my mind.
Jesus, though I cannot see Your hand,
Your faithful heart I understand.
